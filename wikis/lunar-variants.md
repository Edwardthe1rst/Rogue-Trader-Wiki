Many Imperial [Cruisers](hulls-overview.md) share certain similarities in design, and this is particularly true of the Lunar-class cruiser. In fact, some other classes of [Cruiser](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) (the Dictator being a prominent [Example](rules-tests.md)) are built by retrofitting damaged Lunars.

In The Calixis Sector, two particular classes of cruiser-the Dominator and the Gothic, share a great deal in common with the Lunar. Their primary differences lie not in their base Characteristics, but the [Weapons](weapons-general.md) and other Components that they mount. While they do not actually use the same hulls, they are very similar.
