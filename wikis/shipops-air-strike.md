The  Explorer  calls  in  a  pinpoint  air  strike  from  friendly  air forces,  taking  out  specific  targets  such  as  hardened  bunkers, counterattacking tanks, or enemy artillery . In the simple version of this, a single Explorer on the ground must pass a Challenging (+0) Navigation (Surface) Test to determine the coordinates of  the  strike,  then  a Challenging  (+0)  Command  Test to direct the aircraft to the target. In the medium version, a second Explorer is piloting the lead strike craft, and makes an [Attack](combat-attack-rules.md) [Run](rules-combat-overview.md) on the target making a Challenging (+0) Pilot Test for  the attack run, then attacking and rolling [Damage](character-injury.md) with the [Weapons](weapons-general.md) his strike craft possesses against a suitable target (such as a vehicle or  emplacement).  He  gains  a  +5  bonus  to  the  Pilot  Test  and the  attack  rolls  for  every  degree  of  success  his  partner  on  the ground scored on his Navigation (Surface) Test. If the target is eliminated, the enemy unit targeted by this Flash-point rolls 2 less damage dice during the current Strategic Round, and may lose unit Strength at the GM's discretion.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 136`
