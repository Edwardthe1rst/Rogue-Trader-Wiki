Some feudal cultures have the unpleasant habit of preparing shot  with  vermin  poison,  deadly  spores,  or  worse.  Many travellers in the Expanse adopt this practice with more modernweaponry,  with  horrifying  results.  Of  course,  utilising  such dangerous substances can be just as risky to the wielder.

Effects: The weapon gains the Toxic special quality. If the weapon ever jams, the wielder takes 1d5 damage, ignoring Armour and Toughness.

Used With: Any Solid Projectile and Flame Weapons.



*Source:* `Into the Storm, page 129`
