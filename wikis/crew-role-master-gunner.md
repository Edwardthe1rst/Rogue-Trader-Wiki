The Master Gunner is a high-ranking warrant officer given responsibility  for  the  maintenance  and  performance  of  the ship's [Macrobatteries](starship-supplemental-components.md) and lances. On small ships the Master Gunner may inspect each weapon personally, while on larger vessels he is likely to delegate this to several aides. He also is responsible for [Ammunition](economy-wealth-and-acquisitions.md) storage and power linkages.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 72`
