These explosives are the standard armament for Imperial [Torpedoes](weapons-torpedoes.md). These systems are designed to blast through a spaceship's armoured [Hull](starship-anatomy-detailed.md), using their high speed to punch deep into the target vessel. The [Plasma](weapons-general.md) reactor that powers a torpedo's drive forces it deep into the bowels of the ship and then overloads, contributing to the fury of the warhead's detonation. This reduces complexity , making their manufacture comparatively simple. Thus, these torpedoes are the staple of Imperial Navy warships.

Terminal  Penetration  (3): When  rolling  [Damage](character-injury.md)  for  this weapon, re-roll any dice results of 3 or lower. The results of the second roll stand.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 9`
