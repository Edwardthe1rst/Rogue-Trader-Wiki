See page 86 for full rules.## [Complications and Special Rules](warp-space-hulks.md)

Limited Resources: Shadowhunters are designed to operate in close proximity to their Craftworld with limited crews and normally only stock about a month's worth of supplies. In addition, Shadowhunters may not initiate boarding actions or [Hit and Run](starship-combat-rules.md) Attacks. Small and Agile: Due  to  their  small  [Size](character-traits.md)  and  phenomenal  manoeuvrability,  Shadowhunters  are  capable  of  attacking  and destroying [Torpedoes](weapons-torpedoes.md) and [Attack Craft](attack-craft-rules.md). A Shadowhunter ignores penalties for firing on such targets.

## Modifier Summery

The following modifiers apply to the Shadowhunter:

- -1 Movement if heading towards the nearest sun, +1 Movement if at right angle, no effect if moving away.
- +10 to any Command Tests that are part of a Boarding Action.
- Add 1 to Crew Population loss suffered.
- Subtract 1 from Morale loss suffered. (to a minimum of 1).
- -40 on any Test to hit the ship with [Lances](starship-supplemental-components.md), [Torpedoes](weapons-torpedoes.md), [Attack Craft](attack-craft-rules.md) or by ramming. -20 to hit the ship with macrobatteries.
- -30 on any Extended Action involving Detection.
- +10 to all Ballistics Tests involving the Starcannon Cluster Battery .

*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, pages 92–93`
