## Starting Experience

All characters begin play with 4,500 xp. This initial amount of xp is considered to have already been spent in preparing your character's abilities and Skills to the point where they are now. You do not actually spend these xp; that amount represents your character's  pre-existing  starting  [Characteristics](starship-anatomy-detailed.md),  Origin  Path,  Skills, and other abilities. This initial xp is primarily of concern when bringing daRk  HeResy characters  into Rogue  TRadeR. (A  Rank  5 daRk  HeResy character  with  5,000  xp  is  the equivalent of a Rank 1 Rogue TRadeR character with 5,000 xp. See page 34 for more information.) Beginning characters also  have  an  additional  500  xp  that  they  may  spend  to increase  [Characteristics](starship-anatomy-detailed.md),  acquire  or  improve  Skills,  acquire new Talents, and so on. Chapter Ii: Career Paths describes the cost of these improvements, referred to as Advances, as well  as  what  Advances  are  available  based  on  your  [Career](chargen-stage2-origin-path.md) Path. Be sure to note how much xp you spend, as spent xp determines  your  character's  Rank  within  his  [Career](chargen-stage2-origin-path.md)  Path. When you have finished spending your initial 500 xp, your character's total xp spent should equal 5,000.
