'There is no peace amongst the stars.'

- Ancient Proverb

I n the 41st Millennium, the business of [Waging War](mass-combat-waging-war.md) is just like that of any other endeavour, and Rogue Traders step on to the field of battle just as easily as they step into a governor's palace  or  the  grand  staterooms  of  voidships.  Warfare endeavours are an expansion of the standard endeavour rules as  described  in  Rogue  Trader.  They  are  designed  to  grant a  Game  Master  and  his  players  an  immersive  and  detailed playing experience. These [Endeavours](economy-endeavours.md) encompass a player-[Run](rules-combat-overview.md) armed conflict from the break-down of negotiations, through planning, and all the way to the day when the players stand among the smouldering ruins of their enemy's capital.
