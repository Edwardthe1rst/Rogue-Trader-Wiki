Although  the  Rak'Gol  seem  able  to  breath  oxygen,  the atmospheres  aboard  their  ships  are  thick  and  choked  with noxious chemicals, as well as radiation from their unshielded fission  piles.  This  has  lead  xenostechnologists  to  derisively dub their sustainer systems 'fume sustainers.' Likewise, their living quarters are hot, dry, and awash in radiation.
