Using the unit's vox equipment, the Explorer is able to disrupt the  communications  of  the  enemy  unit.  With  a  successful Hard  (-20)  Tech-Use  Test,  the  enemy's  communications have  been  sufficiently  disrupted.  On  the  next  Strategic Round the disrupted unit is at -10 to Tests due to lack of communications  and  [Combat](rules-combat-overview.md)  effectiveness.  Failure  means that  the  enemy  comms  were  strong  enough  to  resist  the Explorer's jamming attempts, and the enemy unit suffers no ill-effects. Of course, a canny vox operator on the other end of a jamming attempt could tell that he's being jammed and trace the signal back to its source. The GM should feel free to create a suitably difficult complication arising from a botched jamming attempt if he should see fit.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 136`
