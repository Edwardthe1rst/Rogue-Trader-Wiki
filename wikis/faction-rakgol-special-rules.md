All Rak'Gol ships are subject to the following [Special Rules](mass-combat-special-rules.md): [Unwieldy](weapons-general.md) (Stutter and Burst [Drives](components-drives.md)): A ship using this drive suffers a -10 to their manoeuvrability (included in the ship's profile) Unshielded  (Stutter  and  Burst  drives): Ships  equipped  with this drive suffer a -10 to any Silent Running attempts. When any attempts are made to detect the vessel (any actions that use a ship's Detection,  such  as  the  Active  [Augury](psychic-disciplines-list.md)  Extended  Action),  treat  the equipped starship as if it were 5 VU closer to the scanning vessel. Rad  fumes: Any  unprotected  non-Rak'Gol  character  within  a Rak'Gol vessel must take a Toughness Test every hour. Failure deals an  immediate  1d10  points  of  Energy  [Damage](character-injury.md)  with  no  reduction from [Armour](armour.md) (unless specifically designed to resist radiation).
