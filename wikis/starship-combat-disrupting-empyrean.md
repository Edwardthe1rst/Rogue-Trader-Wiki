Ground Vehicle: This vehicle follows all rules for ground vehicles.

Open-topped: Enemies may target the crew and passengers of the vehicle by using a Called Shot Attack Action.

Kustom Bitz: (Any one): Armour Plates (Facing Any, +3 AP to assigned facing), Red Paint Job (At the start of each Round roll 1D10, adding the result to the Warbike's Tactical Movement).

Bullet Spray: The driver of this vehicle gains the Auto-stabilized Trait for firing this vehicle's weapons. If this vehicle moved in the same turn that it fired, it does not gain the usual bonus to hit for full-auto fire.

Availability: Scarce
