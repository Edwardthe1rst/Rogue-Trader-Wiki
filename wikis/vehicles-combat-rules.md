A Land Speeder or motorbike only needs one driver, a shuttle requires a pilot and co-pilot, and a battle tank often requires a crew of at least four. The crew section indicates how many people  are  needed  to  run  the  vehicle  and  their  tasks.  The passenger  number  and  cargo  capacity  indicates  how  many passengers or how much weight the vehicle can handle. These numbers should be considered 'rough' estimates, and the GM should determine whether players may cram one more person in their double-seat skimmer, or how many barrels of promethium will fit in the passenger compartment of their shuttle.
