Shooting at squadrons must be resolved in a slightly different way  than  firing  at  individual  vessels.  When  firing  at  a squadron, the primary target will always be the vessel in the

squadron that is closest to the firing ship.

When firing a weapon, the first hit scored must be against the primary target. Any additional hits scored may be allocated against any other vessel in the squadron, so long as at least half of the total hits scored by that Weapon Component are

allocated to the primary target.

With hits scored from [Macrobatteries](starship-supplemental-components.md), the [Damage](character-injury.md) rolls are added together as usual after being allocated to a target vessel, with all the hits allocated to a single vessel being totalled. With hits scored from lances, the damage rolls are resolved

individually as normal.

Due to the way [Torpedoes](weapons-torpedoes.md), [Attack Craft](attack-craft-rules.md), and [Nova Cannons](weapons-nova-cannons.md) [Attack](combat-attack-rules.md)-relying  on  actual  physical  location-all  torpedoes, attack  craft  and  nova  cannons  will  attack  whichever  vessel they pass within range of, regardless of whether or not they

are part of a squadron.

Ships  suffer  damage  and  critical  hits  individually,  but resolved

damage  to Crew  Population and Morale are collectively for the entire squadron.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 114`
