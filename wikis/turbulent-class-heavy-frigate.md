Imported from the Scarus Sector for use as heavy naval escorts during the early days of the Angevin Crusade, the handful of Turbulent-class  heavy  [Frigates](hulls-overview.md)  surviving  in  the  Calixis  Sector never  returned,  some  say  because  they  found  the  tumultuous Calixian region  more to their tastes. Intended to drive ahead of the main fleet and engage [Rival](talents-descriptions.md) scouting units when encountered, the  history  of  the  Turbulent  class  has  been  characterised  by aggressive  and  piratical  actions  against  the  Emperor's  foes. Although rare, the Turbulent has gained a reputation as a 'lucky' class. Turbulents have participated in many of the most glorious naval actions of the sector, whilst coincidentally either surviving or not being involved at all in the most disastrous.

Some  armchair  admirals  dismiss  the  Turbulent,  citing  its comparatively inefficient energy weapon transfer systems and deriding  its  antiquated  internal  [Communication](rules-communication.md)  network  of brass [Vacuum](character-injury.md) pipes and electric lights. However such mockery is  best  done  behind  closed  doors,  as  several  of  the  senior admirals in Battlefleet Calixis served aboard these feisty ships.
