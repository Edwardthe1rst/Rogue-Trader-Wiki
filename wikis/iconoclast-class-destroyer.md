Iconoclasts are a favoured vessel for many pirates due to its firepower and rugged and common design. Iconoclasts are also easy to modify to look like a wide variety of other ships, meaning that they excel at ambushes and striking from [Surprise](combat-surprise-rules.md). Relatively cheap to acquire or repair, Iconoclasts are a common sight in many raiding bands of [Chaos Reavers](ships-chaos-reavers.md).

To include Chaos Iconoclasts, use the [Hull](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) found on page 28, and include the following Essential and [Supplemental Components](wraithship.md#supplemental-components). The Essential Components will not change its profile, but will give the all the Essential Components it requires to operate, 1 Void Shield, and 100 Crew Population and 100 Morale. This version of the Iconoclast has no [Special Rules](mass-combat-special-rules.md).

Essential Components: [Jovian Pattern Class 2 Drive](starship-essential-components.md), Strelov 1 Warp Engine, Gellar Field, Single Void Shield Array, Pirate Bridge, L-12.b Life Sustainer, Pirate Quarters, M-100 Augur Array.

Supplemental Components: 2 Dorsal Macrocannon Batteries: (Macrobattery, Strength 3; [Damage](character-injury.md) 1d10+2; Crit Rating 6; Range 6), Reaver Hold (if this ship is captured with this Component intact, the Explorers gain 20 [Achievement Points](economy-endeavours.md) towards their current Objective.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 111`
