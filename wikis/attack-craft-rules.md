To complement their offensive weaponry, many Eldar vessels also carry various forms of attack craft. These include Darkstar fighters,  which  often  serve  as  escorts  for  Eagle  bombers. For  atmospheric  operations,  the  Eldar  utilise  Nightwing fighters  and  Phoenix  ground-[Attack](combat-attack-rules.md)  craft.  When  the  time comes to transport troops, the Vampire raider is brought into play,  along  with  the  heavily-armed  Vampire  Hunter.  Eldar starfighters are among the most advanced in the galaxy; the Darkstar is easily able to outrun and outmanoeuvre anything fielded by The Imperial Navy. In addition, Eldar attack craft possess  holofields,  making  them  nearly  impossible  to  hit when they make their attack runs. When coupled with their [Armour](armour.md)-cracking sonic charges, this makes the Eagle one of the most feared bombers in space.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 86`
