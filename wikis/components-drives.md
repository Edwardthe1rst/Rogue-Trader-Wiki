Instead of the [Plasma Drives](components-plasma-drives.md) of humanity, [The Rak'gol](faction-rakgol-overview.md) rely on  something  referred  to  as  a  'fission-pulse'  drive.  These drives are far inferior to the plasma drives of Imperial vessels, based around an atomic reactor core and relying on explosive 'pulsed' fission [Reactions](rules-combat-overview.md) to move their ships through space. In general they can accelerate to a decent speed, but fall far short on manoeuvrability.

There are two broad classes of drives used by the Rak'Gol, categorised by xenostechnologists as the 'Stutter' class and the 'Burst' class, with the latter being the larger of the two. Like the smaller 'Stutter' class drives, the 'Burst' drives of the  Rak'Gol  are  capable  of  accelerating  to  high  velocities, but are incapable of nimble manoeuvring. However, Imperial [Captains](imperial-starship-types.md) tend to underestimate the brutal force behind them, sometimes to their peril.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 98`
