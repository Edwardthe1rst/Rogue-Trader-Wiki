Only a very few of the major Forge Worlds maintain the capacity to manufacture seeking guidance systems. [Torpedoes](weapons-torpedoes.md) equipped with these systems employ adaptive cogitators and logic engines that  continuously  analyse  their  target's  [Defensive](weapons-general.md)  actions.  The combination of their rarity and [Combat](rules-combat-overview.md) effectiveness has granted these  torpedoes  a  near-legendary  status. Seeking  Torpedoes grant the torpedo a Torpedo Rating of +30.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 10`
