The  Eldar  disdain  using  [Shells](weapons-ammunition.md)  and  crude  [Warheads](weapons-warheads.md)  on  their starships,  preferring  elegant  and deadly laser and [Plasma](weapons-general.md)-based macrocannons. These potent weapons are devastatingly accurate.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 86`
