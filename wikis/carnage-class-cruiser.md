[Hull](starship-anatomy-detailed.md): Cruiser

Class: Carnage Class

Dimensions: 5 km long, .8 km abeam approx.

Mass: 28.5 Megatonnes approx.

Crew: 80,000 crew approx.

Accel. 2.4 Gravities max acceleration.

Designed as a fleet support vessel, the Carnage class was meant to provide supporting fire from [Long Range](combat-special-circumstances.md) for other ships. Unfortunately, the Carnage suffered from numerous problems during its construction, including many  technical difficulties with the power systems for its long-ranged phased [Plasma](weapons-general.md) batteries. These issues with the Carnage class caused raging debate amongst Battlefleet Obscura, dividing scores of influential [Captains](imperial-starship-types.md) into opposing camps. Although these challenges were eventually overcome, numerous Carnage-class [Cruisers](hulls-overview.md) have turned traitor, beginning with the very first vessel of its kind. It is rumoured that there is some bizarre, maddening twist in the ship's design that leads its crews inevitably towards discord and mutiny . Carnage cruisers are feared opponents, able to cripple enemy ships with punishing salvos of long-range battery fire.
