Smaller  void  ships  only  carry  a  handful  of  [Astropaths](psychic-psyker-types.md)  but capital  ships  commonly boast an entire choir for long-range [Communication](rules-communication.md). Regardless of [Size](character-traits.md) one of the astropaths on the ship is always designated as the chief astropath and has direct dealings with the [Captain](rank-captain.md). Much of what passes between them is by necessity kept secret from the rest of the ship so the chief astropath often becomes the captain's closest confidant.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 72`
