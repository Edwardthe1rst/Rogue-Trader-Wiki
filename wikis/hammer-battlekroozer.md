## Using the Hammer

All  the  guidelines  that  apply  to  using  Kill Kroozers apply to the Hammer as well, with the cautionary note that it is in every way a superior vessel.  The  bombardment  cannons  make  the  ship especially  potent,  and  it  can  carry  enough  fightabommerz  to  protect  itself  against  [Attack](combat-attack-rules.md)  craft.  This ship  should  be  reserved  as  a  nemesis  for  a  group  of Explorers, and is a challenging foe to defeat.

Certainly, the unique weapon system on the Hammer's prow was invented by Garzog, due to Uzog's insane desire to have both [Torpedoes](weapons-torpedoes.md) and big guns on his vessel. Garzog is said to have rigged the Nova Cannon's conduits to massive cylindrical launchas that could fire torpedoes or bombardment cannon [Shells](weapons-ammunition.md), but not both at once. Since he got the design half right, Uzog is said to have only ripped one of his arms off.

Whatever its origins, the Hammer is a feared and hated vessel in the Expanse. There is only one known Hammer, the [Dethburna](weapons-ork-dethburna.md) , and the Navy is known to rate only Da Wurldbreaka itself as more dangerous.
