Drop Pod Assault: Drop pods are somewhat unique amongst vehicles, in that once they 'land,' they do not move under their own power. Pods must be launched from a starship with drop pod launch bays and travel directly downward towards the target planet until they hit the ground. They cannot engage in manoeuvres, although provided they are at least 500 km above the planet's surface, their destination may be changed in-flight to any location within 25 km of the original destination. All shooting at a drop pod suffers a -30 to hit when it is in flight. When landing, the drop pod picks a set destination, then scatters 2d10 metres in a random direction. This is the final location of the pod, which cannot move until recovered and returned to its parent ship.
