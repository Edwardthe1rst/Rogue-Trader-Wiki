Slower then the Darkstar, Eagle bombers use a modified version of the same holofield generator carried by capital ships to protect them from enemy sensor and targeting systems. Although armed with starcannons for protection against other [Attack Craft](attack-craft-rules.md), their true [Weapons](weapons-general.md) are the highly destructive sonic charges they carry. These charges are delivered with pinpoint accuracy due to [The Eldar](faction-eldar-overview.md)'s highly sophisticated targeting systems. When they impact, they release a shattering seismic blast whose shockwaves can rupture metres of [Armour](armour.md). The Eagle is manoeuvrable enough that it can survive dogfights with enemy fighters.
