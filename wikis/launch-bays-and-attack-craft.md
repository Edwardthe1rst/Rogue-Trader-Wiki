Carriers open up a whole variety of tactics to a vessel, but also add a lot of complication. It takes [Extended Actions](starship-combat-rules.md) and/or [Orders](combat-orders.md) to use [Attack Craft](attack-craft-rules.md), drawing upon the limited resource of  character  actions  that  may  often  be  needed  elsewhere. However, the [Damage](character-injury.md) potential of assault boats and bombers, and the [Defensive](weapons-general.md) capabilities afforded by fighters, can make this expenditure worthwhile.

[Attack](combat-attack-rules.md)  Craft  are  versatile  and  potent  assets.  Bombers  and assault boats can deal considerable amounts of damage, including multiple critical hits, while fighters can negate the effectiveness of [Torpedoes](weapons-torpedoes.md) and the otherwise-deadly attack craft.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 118`
