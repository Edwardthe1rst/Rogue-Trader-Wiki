During  [Combat](rules-combat-overview.md),  these  bays  are  completely  open  to  the  void. Docked assault  craft  are  each  attached  to  an  airlock  which is  used  to  load  the  vessels.  In  non-combat  situations, massive doors are closed to seal off the Component so that  the  area  may  be  used  to  conduct  repairs  and maintenance-inasmuch as [The Rak'gol](faction-rakgol-overview.md) engage in these activities.
