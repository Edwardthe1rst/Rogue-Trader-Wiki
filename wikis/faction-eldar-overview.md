- Corsair [Captain](rank-captain.md) Shibán Nyssyn to a foe.

P ossibly the oldest space-faring race in the galaxy , the Eldar were travelling among the stars while mankind was still learning to walk upright. Close to human in appearance, Eldar tend to be taller and slimmer than the average Imperial citizen, with sharply-angled features and distinctive pointed ears. They are best known for the sublime Grace of their movements as well as possessing blindingly fast reflexes. A tremendously longlived people, with life-spans measured in centuries, the Eldar are also  a  slowly  dwindling  race.  Their  homeworld  was  lost  long ago during the catastrophic collapse of the Eldar civilisation, and they  are  now  scattered  across  the  galaxy ,  mostly  dwelling  on planet-sized spaceships known as Craftworlds.
