Cavalry units are fielded by feral, feudal, and some industrial level societies. They are composed of individual soldiers mounted on beasts or horses. Cavalry units are fast and manoeuvrable and are skilled at lightning strikes, raiding and harrying enemy forces, and causing mayhem on the battlefield.

Light  Cavalry: Much  like  their  light  infantry  cousins,  light cavalry units tend to be [Raiders](ships-raiders-overview.md), skirmishers, and scouts. They stay out of the centre of a battle, harrying flanks with las-carbines and sabres, and reporting information to their superiors.

Medium Cavalry: More heavily armed and armoured than their lighter cousins, medium cavalry are the main line troops of any cav  squadron.  These  troops  make  up  the  bulk  of  cavalry ,  and tasked with the workaday [Combat](rules-combat-overview.md) missions undertaken by any unit.  Medium cavalry units will be mounted on heavier beasts or mounts where the beast itself is a weapon. Perhaps the most famous medium cavalry units are the Death Riders of the wartorn world of Krieg and their vat-grown mounts.

Heavy Cavalry: Truly  a  sight  to  behold,  heavy  cavalry  are the shock troops of a cavalry squadron. Heavy cavalry will be mounted  on  heavily-armoured  chargers  or  other  beasts,  and carry powerful weaponry and [Armour](armour.md). Heavy cavalry units even employ giant beasts topped with fighting platforms that can carry a dozen or more troops. The nigh-mythical Thunderwolf Cavalry of the Space Wolves are one such [Example](rules-tests.md).
