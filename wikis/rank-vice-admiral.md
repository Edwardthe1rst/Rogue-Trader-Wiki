By  long  tradition  a  vice-admiral  commands  the  leading division  of  the  fleet,  the  part  that  would  equate  to  the vanguard of a terrestrial force. In later times this has come to mean commanding a force of [Light Cruisers](ships-light-cruisers-overview.md) and destroyers charged with scouting for the enemy, charting navigational hazards and long-range patrolling. It's common for a viceadmiral to choose a [Light Cruiser](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) as his flagship to better keep pace with his far-flung [Squadrons](squadrons-overview.md).



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 69`
