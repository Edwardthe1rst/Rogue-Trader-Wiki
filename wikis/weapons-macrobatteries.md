Rapid-firing but imprecise, [Macrobatteries](starship-supplemental-components.md) tend to be good at overwhelming shields and, against unshielded and/or lightlyarmoured targets, good at dealing conventional [Hull](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) Integrity [Damage](character-injury.md). However, against heavily-armoured targets or targets with  considerable  shielding,  it  requires  a  lucky  hit  to  deal any damage beyond removing the shields. The majority of vessels are likely to carry at least one macrobattery, if only for preparing a target by removing [Void Shields](components-void-shields.md).



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 118`
