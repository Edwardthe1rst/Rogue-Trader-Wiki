These vast ships of the line form the iron backbone of the Imperial  battlefleets.  Principal  [Combat](rules-combat-overview.md)  vessels  designed  to pound the enemy into flaming ruin, they are monolithic and awesome vessels only rarely found in the hands of the most wealthy and ambitious Rogue Traders.
