As with several other rules pertaining to starships, the rules for [Critical Hits](starship-combat-rules.md), [Crew Population and Morale](starship-crew-population-morale.md) presented in the Rogue TRadeR core rulebook are primarily designed for the use of player characters. As such, they tend to include details which are largely unnecessary to running [Npc Starships](ships-npc-starships.md).

Simplified methods for resolving Critical Hits and [Damage](character-injury.md) to Crew Population and Morale are presented here.

*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 115`
