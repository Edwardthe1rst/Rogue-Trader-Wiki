A unit that is dug in has settled in for a long fight. They have taken to heavy [Cover](combat-special-circumstances.md), built field fortifications (trenches, banks, revetments,  and  the  like),  and  have  gone  to  ground.  With  a successful Challenging  (+0) Scholastic Lore: Tactica Imperialis  Test ,  the  unit  has  successfully  dug-in  and  are considered to have 10 points of cover. If the unit fails their Test, they still build their fortifications and dig their trenches, but receive only 5 points of cover due to inadequate cover.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 133`
