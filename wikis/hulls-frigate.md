Often  overlooked  as  insignificant  by  technocratic  senior officers obsessed with the raw firepower of true [Battleships](ships-battleships-overview.md)

or ships of the line, [Frigates](hulls-overview.md) offer so much more than pure scouting and escort vessels. These elegant and fast craft travel further, explore more widely and routinely see more [Combat](rules-combat-overview.md) than the larger battlefleet vessels simply by virtue of the vast number of tasks they are capable of performing.
