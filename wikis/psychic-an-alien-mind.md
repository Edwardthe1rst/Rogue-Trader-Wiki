This Kroot was born into a Kindred that has selected prey possessing great courage. Many such Kindreds have feasted upon the apex predators of a dozen of worlds, passing on the gifts  of  increased  bravery,  excellent  hand-eye  coordination, and coolness under fire.

Benefits: The Kroot gains +5 BS, a +5 bonus to Wrangling Tests, and a +10 bonus to resist Fear or Pinning.
