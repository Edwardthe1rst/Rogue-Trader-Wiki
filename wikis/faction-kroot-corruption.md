The Kroot have battled against Orks for centuries, and it was during a major battle on their homeworld of Pech that the Kroot and Tau first formed an alliance. Many times since, the Orks have encountered Kroot mercenaries as both enemies and allies, and the Shapers have learned to strengthen their Kindred  by  devouring  Ork  flesh.  Doing  so  produces  a startling resilience in a Kroot's frame.

Benefits: The  Kroot  gains  +10  Toughness.  This  bonus  is applied after calculating starting Wounds.



*Source:* `Into the Storm, page 50`
