## Special Rules and Modifier Summary

Sheer Momentum :  Warspheres cannot make any special Manoeuvres. Instead each turn they move 2 VU in their current direction. They then may make a Challenging (+0) Pilot (Space Craft)+Manoeuvrability Test. On a success, and for every degree of success, it may move an additional 1 VU in any direction, to a maximum of 3 VUs. If it moves at least 2 VUs, it may change its direction and facing to the direction it is now moving, otherwise it continues moving in the previous direction.

Massively  Redundant :  All  [Components](starship-anatomy-detailed.md)  are  stubbornly  tough  and seem to perform at least four other functions, all networked together in a bewildering system. When a warsphere suffers a Crit, it takes five additional [Damage](character-injury.md) instead. When destroyed, it fragments into a huge cluster of debris. Large Crew: All Crew Pop losses are reduced by 5, to a minimum of 1. Split Firepower : As the keel [Weapons](weapons-general.md) are mounted all over the spherical hull, only half of them at most can be targeted at any one enemy vessel. Planetary Landings :  Warspheres may safely land on a planet as long as they have half their Hull Integrity, and can take off as long they have 75 percent. Having a Warsphere as part of a Military Endeavour against a planetary target also adds 200 [Achievement Points](economy-endeavours.md) towards completing any invasion-related objectives.

## Using Warspheres

Kroot Warspheres are an uncommon sight in the Expanse, but there are

<!-- image -->

several  that  travel  through  the  space  lanes. Warspheres  are  dangerous  opponents,  simply because  they  are  incredibly  tough  and  have a  large  number  of  guns.  However,  due  to the  Kroot's  mercenary  nature,  dealing  with one may be easier (and more profitable) than fighting  it.  Likewise,  the  Kroot  do  not  often hire  themselves  to  take  on  space  born  foes, prefering [Planetary Invasions](mass-combat-planetary-invasion.md) or boarding actions where their talents are not wasted.

In [Combat](rules-combat-overview.md), Warspheres are somewhat unconcerned  with  manoeuvring,  expecting  that most ships can outrun them. Warspheres primarily fight defensively unless trying to break through a blockade to invade a planet, and usually flee if in any serious danger of being overwhelmed.

Kindred : Each Warsphere grants +30 to all Command Tests involving Boarding Actions and [Hit and Run](starship-combat-rules.md) Attacks.
