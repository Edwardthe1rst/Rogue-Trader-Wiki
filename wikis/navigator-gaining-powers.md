I n Rogue  TRadeR ,  Navigator  characters  have  access  to special [Navigator Powers](navigator-powers-list.md) representing the unique gifts of their Lineage. These powers are gained and deepened by spending experience. Each [Navigator Power](talents-descriptions.md) is divided into three  levels:  Novice,  [Adept](rules-allies-enemies-rivals.md)  and  Master.  These  three  levels  reflect a Navigator's mastery over his powers and his understanding of  the  true  nature  of  the  warp.  When  a  Navigator  gains  a new power for the first time ,it will start at the Novice level. Thereafter, at each Rank, they may spend [Experience Points](economy-rewards-measure-of-success.md) to gain either a new power or raise an existing power to the next level (i.e., Novice to [Adept](rules-allies-enemies-rivals.md) or Adept to Master).

See page 60 in Chapter Ii: Career Paths for full details on Navigator advancement.

## Initial Powers

A  Navigator  character  begins  play  with  The  Lidless  Stare power  and  may  choose  one  more  power,  or  instead  of choosing a second power, he may increase his mastery of one of his powers by one stage. At higher ranks a Navigator may spend experience to gain access to additional powers.

The Eye is Open: A Navigator is a living window into [The Warp](warp-imperial-space-travel.md), a fact mercifully mitigated for his own soul's and sanity's sake by the effects of [The Navigator Gene](navigator-gene-rules.md) that allows him to perceive [The Warp](warp-imperial-space-travel.md)'s mind-blasting truth in a unique way that allows his human mind to deal with it. As a result he does not suffer [Corruption Points](character-corruption.md) from [Warp Shock](character-corruption.md) (see page 299), although he can acquire Corruption Points normally by other means.
