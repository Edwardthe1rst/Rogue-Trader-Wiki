Some or all of the user's bones are wrapped with a lattice of  plasteel  to  strengthen  them  and  prevent  damage.  This implant  is  commonly  provided  for  fingers,  forearms,  shins, and ribs both to offer a stronger punch as well as grant more protection in combat. A character with this Implant gains the Bulging Biceps and Iron Jaw Talents, and gains a +2 bonus to Damage for all unarmed attacks.
