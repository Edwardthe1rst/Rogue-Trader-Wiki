The  first  step  in  planning  a  war  is  setting  strategic  goals. These goals are the player's stated [Objectives](economy-endeavours.md) and the desired outcome  of  the  conflict.  Depending  on  its  [Size](character-traits.md),  a  Warfare Endeavour can have 1-5 stated strategic goals.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 139`
