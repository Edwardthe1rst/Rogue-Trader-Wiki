Dreadclaws can be used either as [Boarding Torpedoes](weapons-boarding-torpedoes.md) or Drop Pods for planetary assaults. They are can be launched from [Launch Bays](launch-bays-overview.md) or even fired like an unmanned torpedo, and use ancient [Phase](character-traits.md)-field technology to cut through the thickest [Hull](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) in minutes, deploying their raiding parties before any defence is mustered. Unlike Imperial drop pods, Dreadclaws have the ability to move under their own power even after landing, although they are far slower than during the initial assault.
