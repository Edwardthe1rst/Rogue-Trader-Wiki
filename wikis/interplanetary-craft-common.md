The  following  are  some  common  names  given  by voidfarers for different types of interplanetary craft. Asteroid miners: These range from single-pilot craft right up to semi-mobile gigacomplexes. Booster: A powerful engine with wings for pushing cargo up into orbit, often [Servitor](equipment-tools.md)-controlled. [Brig](components-cargo-passenger.md): A small ship for carrying personnel or prisoners. Courier: A fast ship for small, important cargoes. Cutter: A fast interplanetary vessel, often armed. Hauler: Mid-[Size](character-traits.md) interplanetary cargo vessel. Lander: Winged craft suitable for [Flying](combat-movement.md) in atmosphere. Lighter: A light cargo mover, slow and boxy, with little thought given to any details save cargo moving. Lugger: A heavy cargo mover with powerful thrustengines, suitable for High-G environments. Monitor: A slow but  heavily-armed system defence ship Passenger  liner: A  large  vessel  purpose-built  for carrying  people.  Rare  in  all  but  the  most  populous systems, as few can afford the luxury of space travel. Pinnace: A small ship capable of orbital and interplanetary travel.

Pleasure craft: Yachts and other playthings for the elite.
