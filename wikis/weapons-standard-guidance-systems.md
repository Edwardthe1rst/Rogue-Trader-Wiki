Most  [Torpedoes](weapons-torpedoes.md)  possess  these  machine  spirits,  which  use [Augury](psychic-disciplines-list.md) systems to scan their flight path for ship sized mass readings,  radiation  output,  heat,  and  other  identifiers.  All torpedoes come with a standard guidance system normally, this does not modify their [Availability](economy-availability-rules.md). Standard Guidance Systems grant the torpedo a Torpedo Rating of +20.
