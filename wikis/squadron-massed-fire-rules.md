[Squadrons](squadrons-overview.md)  fire in a different manner  than  lone  ships, representing their ability to fill space with shots. This ability is called Massed Fire. When a squadron performs Massed Fire, select any number of either [Macrobatteries](starship-supplemental-components.md) or lances; the two may not be combined for this purpose. Then select a single one of those [Components](starship-anatomy-detailed.md).

Roll  to  hit  with  that  Component  as  normal.  Every additional Component after the first grants an additional +10 to hit and +1 to the Strength of the Component for that [Shot](weapons-ammunition.md) only (effectively increasing the number of maximum shots by one). The [Damage](character-injury.md), range and Crit Rating are all determined however,  all  Weapon  Components  being  used  must  be  in range of the target. In addition, as only a single [Attack](combat-attack-rules.md) roll is

by the Component for which [The Attack](rules-combat-overview.md) roll is being madebeing made only one Critical Hit can be scored.

Squadrons  can  combine  macrobatteries  or  lances  into multiple  groups  for  purposes  of  Massed  Fire-for  [Example](rules-tests.md), a  squadron  with  eight  macrobatteries  could  fire  make  one Massed Fire with all eight [Weapons](weapons-general.md), two with four each, or

any other combination.

This is intended to speed up combat involving squadrons of [Npc Starships](ships-npc-starships.md), as well as give smaller ships an ability to

work together to take out larger vessels.

*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 114`
