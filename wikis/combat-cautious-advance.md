While speed is  typically  of  the  essence  during  [Combat](rules-combat-overview.md),  sometimes discretion is the better part of valour and a more measured pace is called for. A unit executing a Cautious Advance moves slowly and carefully across  the  battlefield  and  stays  constantly  alert for any threats. During a Cautious Advance, a unit moves half its speed for the Strategic Round, and incurs no penalties if it encounters enemy units. See the '[On the Advance](combat-on-the-advance.md)' sidebar for information regarding moving and fighting.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 132`
