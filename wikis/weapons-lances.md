Compared to [Macrobatteries](starship-supplemental-components.md), lances are powerful and precise but slow-firing. Effective against heavily-armoured targets and more likely to deal crits, a lance is unfortunately somewhat ineffective at overcoming shields, due to its low rate of fire.

Lance-armed  vessels  are  more  effective  when  a  target's shields have been overwhelmed by a macrobattery first; lance shots are too useful and too rare a commodity to waste against an enemy's shields.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 118`
