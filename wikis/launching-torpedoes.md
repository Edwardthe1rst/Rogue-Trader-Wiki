[Torpedoes](weapons-torpedoes.md)  differ  from  [Macrobatteries](starship-supplemental-components.md)  and  lances  in  a  number of ways, particularly in the manner they are fired. For the sake of  clarity ,  the  entire  process  of  launching  and  attacking  with torpedoes is described here and does not refer  to  the  rules  for shooting on pages 219-221 of the Rogue T RadeR core rulebook.

In  all  cases,  torpedo  tubes,  torpedo  launchers  and  other equivalent  [Components](starship-anatomy-detailed.md)  must  always  be  mounted  in  either a  Prow  or  Keel  weapon  location.  The  forces  exerted  upon a vessel when firing a salvo of torpedoes are such that only certain mounts can properly contain them. For this reason, on any vessel of Grand Cruiser [Size](character-traits.md) and smaller, torpedoes may only fire into the Fore arc, regardless of where the weapon Component is located.

*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 7`
