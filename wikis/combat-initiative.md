At the beginning of a strategic round, each unit, or element if using the more detailed element and composition rules,  rolls 1d10 and adds their Characteristic  Bonus  to  determine  its [Initiative](starship-combat-rules.md). Unit [Characteristic Bonuses](chargen-stage1-characteristics.md) are based on the unit's quality level as detailed in Table 4-5: NPC Troop Quality . In  the  case  of  a  unit  with  a  PC  embedded  in  a  leadership position,  the  character  does  not  add  any  of  his  initiative bonuses to that of the unit as a whole. See the sidebar on more information regarding [Surprise](combat-surprise-rules.md) in [Large-scale Warfare](mass-combat-rules.md).
