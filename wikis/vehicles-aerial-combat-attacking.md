In  aerial  combat,  there  are  countless  potential  manoeuvres a  skilled  and  creative  pilot  could  perform.  Manoeuvre  is  a 'catch-all'  manoeuvre  designed  to  represent  anything  a pilot may invent. All Manoeuvres must be approved by the GM (and  in  general,  the  GM  should  use  discretion  when inventing his own manoeuvres), and have a base difficulty of Challenging (+0) , which can be modified depending on the complexity of the Manoeuvre. Potential Manoeuvres include combat landings under enemy fire, 'Split-S' manoeuvres, and dive-bombing attacks.
