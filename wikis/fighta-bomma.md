There is no such thing as a 'standard' design for Ork [Attack Craft](attack-craft-rules.md), although those unfortunate enough to fight them note that they tend to fall into broad patterns. The most common Ork attack craft found on Ork capital ships is referred to by greenskins as the 'fighta-bomma.' To the frustration of those in The Adeptus Mechanicus, fighta-bommas are also used as ground-support aircraft planetside, and as far as anyone can tell, the only difference is space-born fighta-bommas may be slightly bigger. The learned magos are at a loss to explain how something apparently designed for atmospheric operations also works in the void. Fighta-bommas carry large payloads of heavy bombs for anti-ship work, though not as large as dedicated bombers such as the Starhawk.
