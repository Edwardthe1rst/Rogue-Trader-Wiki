Wars can be almost any [Size](character-traits.md), but for ease of gaming, they are  grouped  into  three  categories,  the  brushfire  war,  the [Campaign](rules-campaign.md), and the [Theatre](mass-combat-theatre.md). Each one lists a potential [Profit Factor](economy-wealth-and-acquisitions.md) cost that must be permanently spent to embark on the war, a number of victory points that must be earned, and a reasonable Profit Factor reward if the war is won.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 139`
