While the Kroot do possess two genders, there are no real cultural differences between a male and a female Kroot. Physically, female Kroot are very difficult to distinguish from the males, and most observers find it nearly impossible to tell  which  is  which.  The  Kroot's  unusual  senses  mean  that  the  sexes  are  mostly  distinct  in  ways  unnoticeable  to  a human, and by the same token, most humans appear just as sexless to the Kroot! Non-Kroot attempts to seduce Kroot, and vice versa, always fail.



*Source:* `Into the Storm, page 51`
