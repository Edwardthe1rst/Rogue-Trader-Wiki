This is a representation of how much damage a vehicle can sustain  before  being  destroyed.  Like  Wounds,  Structural Integrity is a fixed number, and damage to Structural Integrity remains on a vehicle until it is repaired.

A  vehicle's  Structural  Integrity  can  be  affected  by  any number of things, from the sturdiness of the materials used to construct the vehicle to the amount of redundancy built into

critical systems.



*Source:* `Into the Storm, page 172`
