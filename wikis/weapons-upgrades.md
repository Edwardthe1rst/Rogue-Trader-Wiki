## Compact

Compact  [Weapons](weapons-general.md)  are  smaller  versions  of  pistol  and  basic [Weapons](weapons-general.md)  favoured  by  those  who  prefer  concealment  over stopping power. This upgrade halves the weight of the weapon but also halves its clip [Size](character-traits.md) and range as well as reducing its [Damage](character-injury.md) by 1. Anyone attempting to find a concealed weapon with  the  Compact  upgrade  suffers  a  -20  penalty  to  any related Skill Test (e.g., Awareness, Search).

## Fire Selector

For [Weapons](weapons-general.md) that can use differing [Ammunition](economy-wealth-and-acquisitions.md) [Rounds](rules-combat-overview.md), a [Shot](weapons-ammunition.md) selector can be fitted for ease of switching from one type to another. A weapon with a fire selector can have up to three different clips added. At the start of the shooter's Turn, he can use his fire selector to choose which clip he wishes to draw ammo from that round.

[Upgrades](components-upgrades.md): (Bolt) and (SP) Pistols or (SP) and (Bolt) Basic [Weapons](weapons-general.md).

## Forearm Weapon Mounting

This  heavy  bracing  allows  a  single  ranged  weapon  to  be mounted  along  the  arm,  with  specific  hand  movements triggering the weapon. It allows the user to keep both hands free, and can have a strong visual impression on their foes as fire seems to appear from the user's outstretched arms. This upgrade reduces the Weapon's range by 30%.

[Upgrades](components-upgrades.md): [Primitive](weapons-general.md), Las, Solid Projectile, Bolt, or Melta pistol.

## Melee Attachment

Bayonets, chain blades, and other long-bladed  [Combat](rules-combat-overview.md) attachments can be fitted to most ranged [Weapons](weapons-general.md), making them  more  useful  in  assaults.  A  weapon  with  a  melee attachment counts as a [Spear](weapons-general.md) in close [Combat](rules-combat-overview.md).

[Upgrades](components-upgrades.md): Any  Basic  ranged  weapon  can  have melee attachments.## Mono

Mono weapons have specially fashioned blades with superfine edges that can easily cut through [Armour](armour.md) and never lose their edge. Mono weapons no longer count as Primitive and add a  +2  bonus  to  their  Penetration.  The  Mono  upgrade  can be  applied  to  a  power  weapon,  but  it  has  no  effect  whilst the  power  field  is  active.  If  the  power  field  is  ever  lost  or deactivated, the Mono upgrade's bonuses will then apply.

Note: The Mono upgrade may be applied to any primitive melee weapon, but when applied to close-combat weapons that  do  not  use  an  edge  (e.g.,  hammers,  mauls,  etc.),  it  is treated  differently.  Game  Masters  are  encouraged  to  come up  with  interesting definitions  for  non-edged  weapons with  the  Mono  upgrade.  For  [Example](rules-tests.md),  a  hammer  with  the Mono upgrade could be defined as having a pneumo-shock enhancement. The in-game effects remain the same.

Upgrades: Any primitive close combat weapon.

## Motion Predictor

A complex target-tracking cogitator in this device is activated once the user has sighted the desired target. The Predictor then  tracks  the  target,  and  when  the  trigger  is  pulled  will chase  the  target  with  all  the  [Rounds](rules-combat-overview.md)  in  a  clip  in  one  long burst.  A  Motion  Predictor  adds  a  +10%  bonus  to  Ballistic Skill Tests, but only functions when the weapon is fired on semi-automatic or fully automatic modes.

[Upgrades](components-upgrades.md): Any non-[Primitive](weapons-general.md) ranged weapon capable of Semi-Auto or Full Auto fire.

## Omni-scope

A  vastly  expensive  and  rare  device  normally  found  only in  the  hands  of  legendary  assassins  or  bounty  hunters,  the Omni-scope is a cluster of finely-tuned sensor wands linked to a precision lens. Some of these scopes plug directly into a bionic cranial port, allowing the user to shoot around corners without exposing himself-the scope becomes a 'third eye.' The Omni-scope combines the benefits of a Preysense Sight, Red-Dot-Laser Sight, and Telescopic Sight.

[Upgrades](components-upgrades.md): Any  Basic-class  Las,  Solid  Projectile,  Bolt, [Primitive](weapons-general.md)  or  [Plasma](weapons-general.md)  weapon.  An  Omni-scope  counts  as  a sight and a gun may only have one sight.

## Overcharge Pack

This is an enhanced power pack for a las weapon that increases the strength of its fire. An overcharge pack adds 1 to the weapon's [Damage](character-injury.md). Unfortunately the increased output of [Damage](character-injury.md) reduces the number of shots and so the clip [Size](character-traits.md) is halved.

[Upgrades](components-upgrades.md): Any Pistol (Las) or Basic weapon (Las).

## Photo Sight

This is a weapon sight that enhances ambient light levels, improving the user's vision and ability to see  in  the  dark.  A  character  using  a  Photo Sight suffers no penalties due to [Darkness](combat-special-circumstances.md).

[Upgrades](components-upgrades.md): Any Basic ranged Las, Solid Projectile, Bolt, [Primitive](weapons-general.md) or [Plasma](weapons-general.md) weapon. A photo sight counts as a sight and a gun may only have one sight.

## Preysense Sight

This is a weapon sight that shows thermal images and reveals the  hiding  places  of  enemies  who  lurk  in  the  [Darkness](combat-special-circumstances.md).  A character using a Preysense sight suffers no penalties due to [Darkness](combat-special-circumstances.md) and gains a +20 bonus to vision-based Perception Tests at night.

[Upgrades](components-upgrades.md): Any Basic ranged Las, Solid Projectile, Bolt, [Primitive](weapons-general.md) or [Plasma](weapons-general.md) weapon. A preysense sight counts as a sight and a gun may only have one sight.

## Red-dot Laser Sight

This is a laser sight that grants a +10 bonus to Ballistic Skill Tests when the weapon is fired on single [Shot](weapons-ammunition.md).

[Upgrades](components-upgrades.md): Any Pistol- or Basic-class Las, Solid Projectile, Bolt, [Primitive](weapons-general.md) or [Plasma](weapons-general.md) weapon. A red-dot laser sight counts as a sight and a gun may only have one sight.

## Silencer

Silencers  lower  the  noise  from  a  weapon's  discharge,  preventing detection  of  the  [Shot](weapons-ammunition.md).  Awareness  Tests  to  hear  shots  made with a silenced weapon suffer an additional -20 penalty and can only be attempted at half the normal distance.

[Upgrades](components-upgrades.md): Stub  revolver,  stub  automatic,  hand  cannon, [Autogun](weapons-general.md), and hunting rifle.

## Suspensors

These  anti-grav  plates  and  studs  attach  to  a  weapon  or equipment, making the use of heavy or cumbersome devices much  easier  by  offsetting  some  of  their  weight.  They  are often used by elite troops on their heavy [Weapons](weapons-general.md). Suspensors reduce the weight of a weapon by one-half. When firing a weapon with suspensors, the operator counts as having the Auto-stabilized Trait-he always counts as braced and may fire the weapon on semi- or full-automatic as a Half Action.

Upgrade: Any Heavy weapon.

## Telescopic Sight

Telescopic sights magnify the target's image making it easy to see. This is especially useful for long-range shots that need to hit a very specific target location. A weapon with a telescopic sight ignores penalties for long and [Extreme Range](combat-special-circumstances.md) as long as the shooter takes a Full Action to [Aim](rules-combat-overview.md).[Upgrades](components-upgrades.md): Any Basic Las, Solid Projectile, Bolt, [Primitive](weapons-general.md) or [Plasma](weapons-general.md) weapon. A telescopic sight counts as a sight and a gun may only have one sight.

## Vox-operated

Specially coded vox receivers are integrated into the weapon's trigger mechanisms, so that they user can operate it by voice if  needed.  Users  can  also  operate  the  [Shot](weapons-ammunition.md)  selector  and switch firing modes where applicable merely by speaking the appropriate command. For security reasons each receiver is usually keyed to just one voice pattern. Additionally, if the weapon is equipped with a fire selector, the operation of that upgrade may also be controlled simply by speaking aloud.

Upgrade: Any  non-[Primitive](weapons-general.md)  Pistol,  Basic,  Grenade,  or Heavy weapon.
