Ork [Warp Travel](warp-imperial-space-travel.md)  is  an  uncertain  and  erratic  process,  and  the warp [Drives](components-drives.md) that allow it are similarly [Unstable](weapons-general.md). Some are rebuilt from the ruins of salvaged [Warp Drives](starship-anatomy-detailed.md), while others are bizarre contraptions built around the disembodied brains of the Ork psykers known as Weirdboyz. Whatever the manner of their construction,  Warp  Engines  all  have  the  same  purpose-to carry a ship into, through, and out of the warp.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 76`
