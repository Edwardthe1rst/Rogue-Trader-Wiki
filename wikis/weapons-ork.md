Above  all  other  [Weapons](weapons-general.md),  Orks  favour  massive  shell-hurling macrocannons,  referred  to  collectively  as  '[Gunz](weapons-ork-gunz.md).'  Grouped together into batteries, these weapons fire volley after volley of solid [Shot](weapons-ammunition.md) and explosive shells, overwhelming any enemy in a torrent of destruction. However, these guns don't always work as desired, sometimes misfiring or jamming. They may also be deactivated for repairs or 'improvements' by a bored mekboy.
