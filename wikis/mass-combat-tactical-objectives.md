Tactical  [Objectives](economy-endeavours.md)  are  the  actual  [Combat](rules-combat-overview.md)  [Components](starship-anatomy-detailed.md)  of  a  W ar Endeavour.  They  are  belligerent  actions  taken  by  the  players directly , or by their proxies, in pursuit of their stated [Strategic Goals](mass-combat-strategic-goals.md). Tactical objectives tend to be rather specific, and involve things like gaining air superiority , disrupting enemy shipping and logistics, sabotaging enemy infrastructure, and full-scale combat.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 139`
