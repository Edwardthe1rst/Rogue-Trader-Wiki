The powerful Gothic class [Cruiser](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) is a familiar sight throughout the Imperium and has a presence in each Segmentum battlefleet. The class carries one of the heaviest lance broadsides in The Imperial Navy, making it a threat to vessels of equal or larger [Size](character-traits.md). However, without the saturating fire of [Macrobatteries](starship-supplemental-components.md), lances are often unable to bring down targets fast enough, meaning the Imperial Navy often pairs Gothics with other [Cruisers](hulls-overview.md) or escort [Squadrons](squadrons-overview.md). These ships bring down an opponent's defences, allowing the Gothic to finish them off. There are several Gothics in The Calixis Sector, including The Gethsemane of Battlefleet Koronus (page 64).

A Gothic differs from a Lunar chiefly in terms of power output. To construct a Gothic, start with the Lunar class hull and equip it with a Jovian-pattern 'Warcruiser' Drive and possibly Auxiliary [Plasma](weapons-general.md) Banks from the InTo The SToRm supplement, Titanforge Lance Batteries in all Port and Starboard weapon capacity slots, [Torpedo Tubes](components-torpedo-tubes.md), and an Armoured Prow Component.  Note, the fearsome lance arrays of a Gothic are hard to construct, meaning a great deal of effort is put into building the ship. When constructing a Gothic, it will likely need several Good [Craftsmanship](components-craftsmanship.md) Components.
