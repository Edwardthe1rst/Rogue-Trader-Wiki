In  many  ways  the  counterpoint  to  [Raiders](ships-raiders-overview.md),  [Frigates](hulls-overview.md)  are  less agile but have better [Armour](armour.md) and greater versatility in their firepower.  Typically  armed  with  dorsal  weaponry,  a  [Frigate](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) can throw fire in almost any direction, and combined with their still-considerable speed and agility, makes them excellent escorts for heavier, slower, or more vulnerable vessels.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 117`
