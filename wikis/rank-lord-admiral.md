A lord-admiral,  or  sector  [Commander](rank-commander.md),  is  responsible  for  all naval operations in a given sector and has direct command of units of the Segmentum warfleet allocated to that sector. Nominally a lord-admiral is based at the Segmentum Fortress with  other  sector-level  administratum  officials,  but  most choose to base themselves at the foremost naval facility of the sector  under their  [Charge](rules-combat-overview.md).  A  lord-admiral  answers  not  only to his superiors in the fleet but to The Adeptus Terra as well. Deploying ships and men to [Patrols](patrols.md), permanent stations and reserve fleets in a way that satisfies both military and civilian needs is a headache most naval officers dread.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 68`
