Many vehicles in the Imperium are armed, ranging from a heavy stubber  on  a  Munitorium  five-tonne  hauler  to  the  multiple banks of forward-firing lascannons aboard a Fury starfighter.

All  vehicle  weapon  entries  will  include  the  statistics  for the weapon, whether it is controlled by the vehicle driver or independently  crewed,  and  its  firing  arc.  The  arc  indicates whether the weapon can fire to the vehicle's front, left side, right side, rear, or any combination thereof.



*Source:* `Into the Storm, page 172`
