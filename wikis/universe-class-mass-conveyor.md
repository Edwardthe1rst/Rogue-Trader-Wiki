Amongst the myriad starships that serve the trade lanes in and  around  the  Calixis  Sector,  some  of  the  largest  are  the mass  conveyors.  Stretching  some  twelve  kilometres  from stem to stern, a mass conveyor appears at a distance to be a slim, elegant craft. However, as one slowly approaches it, this misleading impression is replaced with a horrified awe, as what appear to be radio masts resolve into gigantic towers the [Size](character-traits.md) of hive stacks. The ship's true scale manifests itself as an observer realises that entire cities twinkle and shimmer along its flanks.
