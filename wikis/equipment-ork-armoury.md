Mounted  inside  the  forearm  is  a  long  blade,  which  can spring from a skin vent along the back of the hand to act as  a  shortsword  or  dagger,  an  ideal  surprise  for  a  foe  who believes an individual unarmed. In some versions, the entire hand is augmetic, folding to form a hilt for the revealed blade. The internal blade has the same characteristics as a monoknife, and may gain any weapon upgrades appropriate for its type. Some rare internal blades are power weapons or possess internal  reservoirs  of  poison  with  which  to  coat  the  blade. Good craftmanship Internal Blades possess the Toxic Quality, while Best craftmanship Internal Blades gain +2 damage, +2 Pen, and the Power Field Quality.
