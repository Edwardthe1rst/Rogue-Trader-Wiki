Regular  [Communication](rules-communication.md)  is  required  between  vessels  in  a squadron, using vox and astrotelepathy to ensure that every vessel  knows  what  it's  meant  to  be  doing  at  any  given moment.  The  squadron's  [Commander](rank-commander.md)  must  give  [Orders](combat-orders.md)  to every vessel under his command, and long distances hinder

the necessary swiftness of communication.

Every vessel in the squadron must remain within 3VU of at least one other vessel in the squadron. The entire squadron must form a single unbroken 'chain,' with no gap between vessels of greater than 3 VU. If, for any reason, the squadron formation is broken, the squadron's next [Manoeuvre](rules-combat-overview.md) Action must involve bringing the squadron back into formation-if it wants to remain operational as a squadron. [Squadrons](squadrons-overview.md) cannot operate without a continuous, short line of communication, and are under standing orders to attempt to regroup when those lines are broken.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 114`
