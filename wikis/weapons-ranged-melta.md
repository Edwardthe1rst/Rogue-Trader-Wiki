A straightforward and easy-to-use weapon, the void speargun was designed to tear holes in void suits. Most models use a simple recoilless gas discharge system, but some models are even  simpler  and  use  a  modified  crossbow  often  mounted on the forearm. While not normally deadly on its own, the sudden  rip  of  a  void  suit  generally  will  cause  a  foe  to  be distracted long enough repair the suit integrity lest he suffers decompression. As this weapon is designed to be used with a specific type of ammunition, it may not be equipped with any unusual ammo.
