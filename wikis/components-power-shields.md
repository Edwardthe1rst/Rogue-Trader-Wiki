Orks seem to have possessed [Power Field](weapons-general.md) technology as long as  the  Imperium  has.  Their  ships  sport  protective  energy barriers comparable to [Void Shields](components-void-shields.md), though an Ork generator is  more likely to be covered in crackling electricity, spew clouds of noxious smoke, or randomly vaporize any Orks that stand too close.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 77`
