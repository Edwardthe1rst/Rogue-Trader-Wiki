Agile  but  poorly  armoured,  raiders  are  configured  to  carry potent firepower, but cannot stand up to a great deal of return fire,  forcing  them  to  attempt  to  outmanoeuvre or outrun their enemies. The majority of a raider's firepower is forward-facing, meaning that in order to [Attack](combat-attack-rules.md) successfully , they must close with their targets head-on before breaking off to limit retaliatory fire; essentially , raiders perform long attack runs against their targets.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 117`
