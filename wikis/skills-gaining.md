All newly created Explorers begin with a set of starting skills based on their Home World and chosen [Career](chargen-stage2-origin-path.md) Path. These skills represent what the Explorers knew before starting their current exploits. Over time, Explorers earn [Experience Points](economy-rewards-measure-of-success.md) that can be spent to acquire new skills, or improve existing skills. Exactly which skills can be acquired at a particular time during an Explorer's [Career](chargen-stage2-origin-path.md) is determined by his Career Path.



*Source:* `Roguetrader Corerulebook, page 75`
