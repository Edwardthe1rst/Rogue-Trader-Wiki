The  Imperial  Navy  and  the  Adeptus  Mechanicus  are  closely intertwined, but in shipboard life they often lead entirely separate lives. The tech-priest majoris is the mechanicus representative on the [Bridge](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) crew and communicates the needs of the adeptus to the [Captain](rank-captain.md) and his officers. The tech-priest majoris may not be of especially high rank in the Cult Mechanicus. A common Navy belief is that the most senior Mechanicus adepts on a ship seldom leave the engine rooms, unable to tear themselves away from the throbbing heart of the ship itself.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 72`
