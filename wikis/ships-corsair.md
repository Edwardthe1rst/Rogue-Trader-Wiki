The typical [Eldar Corsair](faction-xenos-overview.md) vessel is the [Size](character-traits.md) of an Imperial [Light Cruiser](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) and as well armed. Equipped with Pulsar [Lances](starship-supplemental-components.md) and holofield-protected [Torpedoes](weapons-torpedoes.md), one such ship can easily shred the average transport-not to mention giving a good account of itself when facing [Raiders](ships-raiders-overview.md) and [Frigates](hulls-overview.md). Of course, most Corsairs never hunt alone, and tend to travel in small groups in order to combine their strengths to make for an even greater whole.
