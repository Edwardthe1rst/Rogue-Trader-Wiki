[The Stryxis](faction-stryxis-overview.md) are an interesting foe for Explorers,  and  are  a  change  of  pace  from  the powerful  and  dangerous  Ork  and  Eldar  ships. Their powerful [Void Shields](components-void-shields.md) make them a relatively durable, though their low Manoeuvrability and  [Armour](armour.md)  means  that  they  can't  stand  up  to sustained fire. However, any interactions with the Stryxis should be based in [Interaction](rules-interaction.md), bluffing, and deceit as much-or more than-in [Combat](rules-combat-overview.md). As a rule, Stryxis ships only [Attack](combat-attack-rules.md) starships that appear weaker than them. If they are confronted with a ship of equal or greater strength, they will either deal, or flee. Also, if they are in danger of being destroyed (10 [Hull](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) Integrity or less) they will flee.

*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 97`
