The Feint is a time-honoured tradition wherein a unit makes a false strike in one location to draw the attention of an enemy unit  away  from  the  true  target.  With  a  successful Difficult (-10) Deceive Test ,  the  unit  is  able  to  completely  fool  the enemy  unit  and  draw  them  away  from  the  real  target.  The attacking  unit  deals  +5  additional  [Damage](character-injury.md)  against  the  real target for 1d5 Strategic [Turns](rules-combat-overview.md) as the enemy finally comes to their  senses  and  scrambles  to  defend  something  they  were unprepared to defend.
