Strangely,  [Weapons](weapons-general.md) are one area where [The Stryxis](faction-stryxis-overview.md) show absolutely no interest in using [Salvage](starship-salvage-rules.md) from other races. They  almost  exclusively  use  their  own  inscrutable technology instead.

The  Stryxis  do  not  utilise  lance  weapons, tending to prefer [Exotic](weapons-ammunition.md) energy weapons thathave an utterly lethal effect on the crews inside starships. These are referred to as 'Ghost-light' macroweapons by those who have encountered  them,  and  are  utterly  reviled  by  most  [Voidsmen](crew-voidsmen.md). Stories abound of corridors filling with a pale gray light-like fog  before  dawn-and  crewmen  keeling  over  dead  without  a mark on their bodies.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 96`
