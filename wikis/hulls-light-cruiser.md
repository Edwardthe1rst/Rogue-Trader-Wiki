Light  [Cruisers](hulls-overview.md)  are  often  characterised  as  scout  vessels,  but some Forge Worlds produce true 'pocket cruisers,' slower, uparmoured and well-armed [Light Cruisers](ships-light-cruisers-overview.md) that offer a cheaper alternative  to  full  cruisers.  They  are  often  easy  to  maintain and less demanding in manpower, and many Rogue Traders find these [Flexible](weapons-general.md) ships extremely attractive.
