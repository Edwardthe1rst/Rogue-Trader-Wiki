To be captain is to become lord and master of all one surveys. The Emperor is the Master of Mankind, but aboard a ship the Captain's very word is His law . Imperial Navy [Captains](imperial-starship-types.md) are aloof, uncompromising figures unbowed by the awesome responsibility entrusted to them. They may be a tyrant, martinet, swashbuckler, strategist  or  saint  but  a  captain  will  always  be  an  exceptional individual to hold full command of an Imperial Navy warship.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 69`
