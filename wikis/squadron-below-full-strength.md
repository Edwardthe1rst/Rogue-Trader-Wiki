Depending  on  the  fortunes  of  war,  ships  may  lose  [Attack Craft](attack-craft-rules.md), and be forced to fight with [Squadrons](squadrons-overview.md) that are at less than full strength. Determining how many craft a squadron is [Missing](combat-special-circumstances.md) depends on how losses to attack craft squadrons are being tracked (see page 16). In any case, squadrons that are half strength or lower only provide the +5 bonus (whether attacking  with  fighters,  bombers,  or  assault  craft)  for  every two additional squadrons, rather than every one.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 12`
