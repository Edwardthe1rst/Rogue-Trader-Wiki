In  many  ways  the  archetypal  grand  [Cruiser](starship-anatomy-detailed.md), the  Avenger  class  is  a  massively  potent and brutally  simple  ship  that  mounts  colossal  close-ranged [Macrobatteries](starship-supplemental-components.md) in broadsides that [Run](rules-combat-overview.md) for kilometres along its flanks. While regarded as unusually [Reliable](weapons-general.md) and faithful for [Grand Cruisers](ships-grand-cruisers-overview.md), the Avengers are now amongst the rarest of their class.

The Avenger dates from an earlier period of fleet tactics, when, [Squadrons](squadrons-overview.md)  of  grand  [Cruisers](hulls-overview.md)  were  employed  as  'line-breakers.' Traditionally , they were thrown into the midst of massive fleet engagements,  soaking  up  enemy  fire  while  racing  into  the middle  of  enemy  formations,  then  crushing  their  opponents at  short  range  with  tetrajoules  of  energy  from  their  oversized broadsides. These unforgiving tactics have taken their toll upon the few surviving Avengers, and they all bear the scars of grim, forgotten battles fought when the Imperium was young.
