Often  rebuilt from  the  largest  broadsides  of  Imperial [Cruisers](hulls-overview.md), these guns protrude menacingly from the scowling armoured  prows  of  kroozers,  [Ready](rules-combat-overview.md)  to  unleash  salvoes  of heavy  explosive  [Shells](weapons-ammunition.md).  Kannonz  often  have  extra-long barrels, so the shells fire further.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 77`
