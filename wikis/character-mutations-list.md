O ne of the most pervasive signs of [Corruption](character-corruption.md) in the Imperium is the manifestation of mutations. These subtle,  or  not-so-subtle,  changes  in  the  flesh  can result  from  exposure  to  [Toxic](weapons-general.md)  pollutants,  alien  biospheres, genetic engineering, radiation, and other hazards, while others might result from exposure to [The Warp](warp-imperial-space-travel.md), [Daemonic](character-traits.md) possession, and other perils of the universe. The level of tolerance for mutants varies planet to planet. Subtle or cosmetic changes are accepted and such beings are regarded as [Abhumans](equipment-abhumans.md) or subs and in some worlds, they form an oppressed and exploited underclass.  The  truly  horrific  mutants  are  purged  on  sight, cleansed with a flamer wherever they are found.

## Gaining Mutations

Characters, creatures, and things acquire mutations in a  variety  of  ways,  but  the  most  common  cause  is  through physical [Corruption](character-corruption.md). Warped environments, chemicals, illegal drugs,  or  just  whimsy  can  all  result  in  mutation,  and  there are few safeguards to prevent a mutation once it takes root. Mutations  function  as  [Traits](character-traits.md),  meaning  characters  cannot normally purchase them with [Experience Points](economy-rewards-measure-of-success.md).
