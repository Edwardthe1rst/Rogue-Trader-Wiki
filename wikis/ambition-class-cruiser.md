The Imperial Navy is by far the most important purchaser of warships in the Imperium. The economies of entire sub Sectors are devoted to supporting the manufacture and supply  of  these  mighty  craft,  principally  in  regions which serve the greater Forge Worlds such as Mars or Cypra Mundi. It is extremely rare for private customers to have the resources to purchase a whole new [Cruiser](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) outright.

The Ambition is representative of such a type of ship. A handful of ships of this rare class have emerged intermittently from  the  orbital  Kormisoshi  Dockyards  of  the  Calixis Sector over the past thousand years, each a phenomenally expensive purchase. They are true [Cruisers](hulls-overview.md), with the ability to pack as much firepower as a Lunar or Tyrant class ship of the Imperial Navy, though they have never been tested in [Combat](rules-combat-overview.md)  against  them-to  anyone's  knowledge.  Invariably incorporating the most luxurious and opulent quarters, the Ambition  class  is  a  briskly  efficient  craft  that  lacks  some of the ultra-conservative redundancies built into true naval vessels.  Some  commentators  have  observed  that  as  such these  ships  are  likely  to  be  somewhat  more  brittle  than many 'true' cruisers, but most Rogue Traders are prepared to sacrifice this for the [Prestige](chargen-stage2-origin-path.md) of owning such a noble and exclusive craft.
