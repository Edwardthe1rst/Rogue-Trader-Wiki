Like ship's crews, soldiers come in a variety of qualities that determine both their modifier and the average level of their skills and [Characteristics](starship-anatomy-detailed.md). This skill level and morale is indicated in Table 4-5: NPC Troop Quality .

Conscript: The lowest of the low, Conscripts are farmers, [Scum](rules-allies-enemies-rivals.md), criminals, madmen, and the poor and destitute of a thousand worlds. Used commonly as cannon fodder for human wave-type attacks, they have the poorest Characteristics and morale, and are given the meanest training, a [Lasgun](weapons-general.md), and sent into the jaws of death. Conscripts are found in every army, such as the the penal legions of The Imperial Guard.

Tithed: A step up from Conscripts, tithed soldiers often make up the bulk of planetary defence forces, Imperial Guard, and Rogue Trader House Troops throughout the Imperium. They can be volunteers or draftees, but are are generally better trained and equipped, and are relatively capable combatants. Their morale is slightly better than Conscripts, but not much and can be broken easily by the [Fear](character-fear-and-damnation.md) and confusion of heavy [Combat](rules-combat-overview.md).

Professional: Professionals are soldiers who have seen their share of combat and have made the waging of war their chosen  profession.  Professionals  compose  the  junior  officers  and  more  decorated  and  experienced  units  within  the Imperial Guard, as well as many mercenary companies.

Veteran: Veterans have fought countless battles, lost friends and brothers-in-arms, and have seen the best and worst humanity has to offer, becoming some of the best warriors and soldiers on the field of battle. Company grade officers and elite units within the Imperial Guard are often Veterans. Their morale is quite high and near unbreakable.

Elite: The Adeptus Astartes, feared and highly respected Storm Trooper regiments, and the very finest mercenaries are all examples of Elite forces. They are the most respected, best cared for, best equipped, hardest hitting, and most expensive armed forces on a field of battle. They have the very highest morale and are nearly impossible to rattle.
