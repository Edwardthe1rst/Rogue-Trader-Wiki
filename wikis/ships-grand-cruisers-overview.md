Relatively  rare  and  extremely  powerful,  grand  [Cruisers](hulls-overview.md)  as the Imperium employs them tend to be capable of far more potent broadsides at the cost of a relative lack of versatility, lacking any weaponry capable of firing in more than one arc, and lacking any prow armament.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 117`
