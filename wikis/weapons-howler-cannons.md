These [Weapons](weapons-general.md) were nicknamed by the survivors of Rak'Gol attacks, who described the sound of their [Shells](weapons-ammunition.md) impacting a ship's [Hull](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) as an 'unrelenting howl.' Howler Cannon batteries fire massive and brutal barrages of ordinance to overwhelm their targets with shear volume of fire.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 98`
