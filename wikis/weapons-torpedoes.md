Superficially similar to [Macrobatteries](starship-supplemental-components.md) in some ways, torpedoes deal considerable [Damage](character-injury.md) and completely ignore [Void Shields](components-void-shields.md). However, the true value of torpedoes is in area denial. Due to the indirect nature of a torpedo [Attack](combat-attack-rules.md) - where the [Weapons](weapons-general.md) travel  across  the  'battlefield'-the  threat  of  an  attack  is  as valuable as the actual damage, as it can deter an enemy from moving in a particular direction.

This technique of 'torpedo herding,' of course, is reliant either on players being appropriately considerate of risk, or upon  the  GM  roleplaying  enemies  appropriately-reckless foes like Orks, for [Example](rules-tests.md), are unlikely to care about enemy torpedoes quite as much as more cautious foes.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 118`
