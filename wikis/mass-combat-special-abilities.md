## Rogue Trader Special Ability

Exceptional Leader: As a free action once per round, the Rogue Trader may grant an ally that he can see and who can hear him +10% to any one test.

## Arch-militant Special Ability

Weapon  Master: Choose  one  (and  only  one!)  class  of weapon. The [Arch-militant](career-arch-militant.md) gains a +10% bonus to hit, +2 to [Damage](character-injury.md), and +2 [Initiative](starship-combat-rules.md) when using a weapon of his chosen class in [Combat](rules-combat-overview.md).

## Astropath Transcendent Special Abilities

[Soul-bound](character-traits.md) to the Emperor: An [Astropath Transcendent](career-astropath-transcendent.md) is [Soul-bound](character-traits.md) to the Emperor during a complex ritual on Terra. An [Astropath Transcendent](career-astropath-transcendent.md) gains +20 to his Willpower when resisting Possession, in any opposed Willpower Test against a  daemon,  or  whenever  making  a  Willpower  Test  to  resist any  Talent,  Psychic  Power,  special  ability,  or  other  effect originating from a daemon. An Astropath Transcendent rolls an  additional  d10  when  rolling  on  the  Perils  of  the  Warp table  and  may  discard  any  one  d10  for  a  more  favourable result.

Psychic  Powers: An  Astropath  Transcendent  begins  play with  access  to  techniques  from  the  Telepathic  Discipline. Astropath Transcendent characters also begin play with the technique  Astral  Telepathy  (see  page  162)  and  may  select two  additional  techniques  from  the  Telepathic  Discipline. [Astropaths](psychic-psyker-types.md) Transcendent begin with a [Psy Rating](talents-descriptions.md) of 2.

See Without Eyes: An Astropath Transcendent is [Blind](weapons-general.md) yet at  the  same  time  has  a  strange  ability  to  perceive  without using  his  physical  senses.  An  Astropath  Transcendent  is functionally  treated  as  if  he  can  see  normally  (including seeing  colours  and  being  limited  by  walls,  etc.).  However, [Astropaths](psychic-psyker-types.md) Transcendent are not affected by effects that target their  vision,  such  as  [Blind](weapons-general.md)  grenades  and  cameleoline.  They are,  however,  completely  incapable  of  seeing  Untouchables (see page 156).

## Explorator Special Ability

[Explorator](career-explorator.md)  Implants: The  [Explorator](career-explorator.md)  begins  play  with the  Mechanicus  Implants  Trait  (see  page  366)  and  may select  up  to  two  additional  common-[Craftsmanship](components-craftsmanship.md)  bionic implants (see page 147). He may spend 200 xp to increase the [Craftsmanship](components-craftsmanship.md) of one of his bionic implants by one level (i.e.,  increasing  from  common  to  good  costs  200  xp).  The Explorator may increase the Craftsmanship up to twice (i.e, taking a common-Craftsmanship to good, then good to best) in this manner, and may only do so at character creation.

## Missionary Special Ability

[Pure Faith](talents-descriptions.md): The Missionary begins play with the [Pure Faith](talents-descriptions.md) Talent. See page 104 for more details.

## Navigator Special Abilities

Warp  Eye: All  [Navigators](psychic-psyker-types.md)  begin  with  the  Lidless  Stare Navigator  power  as  detailed  in Chapter  VII:  [Navigator Powers](navigator-powers-list.md) and  may  either  select  one  additional  power  or improve an existing power. As the Navigator develops, he may select more powers or improve existing powers by selecting the [Navigator Power](talents-descriptions.md) [Advance](combat-advance-action.md).

The Boons of Lineage: A  Navigator hails from a Lineage of a particular type that grants him a number of advantages and quirks. The Lineages which a Navigator may hail from are Nomadic Houses, Magisterial Houses, Shrouded Houses, or Renegade Houses. The details and effects of each of these lineages may be found on pages 176 to 178 in Chapter Vii: Navigator Powers .

Navigator  [Mutations](character-mutations-list.md): The  Navigator  begins  play  with  a number of [Navigator Mutations](navigator-mutations-list.md) determined by the nature of his Lineage.

## Seneschal Special Ability

Seeker of Lore: The [Seneschal](career-seneschal.md) may spend a Fate Point to automatically  succeed  at  any  Ciphers,  Lore,  or  Logic  Test. Doing so  means  that  the  Test  is  resolved  in  the  minimum time  required.  In  addition,  the  [Seneschal](career-seneschal.md)  adds  one  bonus Degree of Success to any successful Commerce, Inquiry, or Evaluate Test.

## Void-master Special Ability

Select one of the following:

Mastery of Space: The [Void-master](career-void-master.md) can re-roll all failed Tests with [Manoeuvre Actions](starship-combat-rules.md) (see page 212) aboard a space ship. Mastery of Gunnery: The [Void-master](career-void-master.md) can re-roll all failed Tests with [Shooting Actions](starship-combat-rules.md) (see page 215) aboard a starship. Mastery of Augurs: The  Void-master  can  re-roll  all  failed Tests involving Detection aboard a starship.

Mastery of [Small Craft](attack-craft-small-craft.md): The Void-master can re-roll all failed Pilot Tests with small craft (shuttles, heavy lifters, guncutters, starfighters, bombers, and so forth).

*Source:* `Roguetrader Corerulebook, page 73`
