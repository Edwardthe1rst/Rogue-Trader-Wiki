Once every unit has taken its Strategic Turn, the Strategic Round ends. [Combat](rules-combat-overview.md) will continue in this way until there is either a clear victory or one side has achieved any [Strategic Goals](mass-combat-strategic-goals.md) it had set. See [Warfare Endeavours](mass-combat-endeavours.md) on page 137 for details on Strategic Goals.

*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 131`
