This  elaborate  document,  written  in  alien  characters  upon unidentifiable xenohide vellum, is a trade agreement signifying the  bearer  as  a  (somewhat)  trusted  trading  partner  of  the nomadic Stryxis. Normally found in the possession of only the most unscrupulous Rogue Traders, the bearer of a Styxian Compact receives a +20 Bonus on all Interaction Skill Tests when  dealing  with  the  Stryxis.  Though  each  Compact  is supposedly issued only to specific humans who have earned the trust of the race, the Stryxis care little about the identity of the bearer, respecting the terms of the Compact regardless of who presents it.



*Source:* `Into the Storm, page 141`
