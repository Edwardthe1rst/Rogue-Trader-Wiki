The Darkstar is a sleek vehicle with the distinctive blade-like wings and divided arrowhead-shaped nose common to Eldar [Attack Craft](attack-craft-rules.md). An extremely fast fighter, it's also extremely manoeuvrable. A sophisticated system of inertia dampeners allows [Turns](rules-combat-overview.md) of nearly 180 degrees, as the crews of many an enemy attack craft have discovered moments before their destruction. They also use a crystal-powered generator that allows for farther-ranging missions than the fighter craft used by the Imperium. Darkstar fighters rely on a less powerful holofield to protect it against incoming fire, rather than thick [Armour](armour.md).
