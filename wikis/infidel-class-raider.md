It is widely believed that the Infidel-class Raider was developed from stolen plans for a larger Imperial escort ship destined to replace the aging Cobra-class Destroyer. The Infidel is a relatively new design, combining a battery of punishing macrocannons with a sizeable array of [Torpedo Tubes](components-torpedo-tubes.md) in the prow. Fast, tough, and incredibly dangerous when formed into [Squadrons](squadrons-overview.md), Infidels quickly  gained  a  dire  reputation  amongst  their  Imperial  Navy  opponents.  Infidels  are  often  used  by  their  vile  [Captains](imperial-starship-types.md)  to perpetrate [Surprise](combat-surprise-rules.md) assaults, leading the way with a wave of [Boarding Torpedoes](weapons-boarding-torpedoes.md) with the ships following close behind [Ready](rules-combat-overview.md) to devastate any survivors with their broadsides.
