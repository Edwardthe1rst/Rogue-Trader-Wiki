The ship's master is generally the ship's most senior warrant officer and most experienced voidsman. Often the master is tasked with critical responsibilities, such as stellar navigation and  the  keeping  of  the  ship's  log.  In  addition,  he  might oversee the keeping of the ship's attitude jets, hanger decks, and stores and supplies.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 70`
