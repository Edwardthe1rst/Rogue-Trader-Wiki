Unlike  most  force  fields  which  absorb  and  defect  attacks, archeotech displacer field devices work by moving the wearer out of harm's way. The wearer can activate the field manually, but  the  device  also  somehow  detects  incoming  attacks  and automatically  activates  a  miniature  warp-jump  engine.  This flicks  the  wearer  out  of  the  Materium  to  avoid  the  attack, restoring him to the Materium some distance away an instant later.  These devices are considered hallowed artefacts by the Calixian Machine Cult, and few are known. Almost all of those are enshrined in forge world temples to the Omnissiah's glory.

If the field blocks an attack, roll for a random direction, then then roll 2d10 for the number of metres travelled-the wearer will always emerge on solid footing and in a suitable empty space. If all three dice come up with the same number (e.g. three sevens), then the user does not re-emerge for 1d5 Rounds and gains 1 Corruption point from exposure to strange energies. If the wearer was Surprised, he must forfeit his next Turn due to the shock. Protection Rating: 55
