Void Dragons are outfitted for extended void operations, with additional supplies and resources.

[Weapons](weapons-general.md): Two Prow Pulsar [Lances](starship-supplemental-components.md), one Prow Starcannon Cluster Battery, two Keel [Landing Bays](components-landing-bays.md).

Holofield: See page 86 for full rules.

Extended Supply Vaults: A Void Dragon may remain at void for up to two years before suffering from [Crew Population and Morale](starship-crew-population-morale.md) loss. In addition, any successful [Extended Repairs](starship-travel-non-combat.md) repair an additional 5 points of [Hull](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) Integrity.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 95`
