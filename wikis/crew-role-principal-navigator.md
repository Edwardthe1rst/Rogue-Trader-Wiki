The most senior Navigator has the terrible responsibility of charting a passage through [The Warp](warp-imperial-space-travel.md) and bringing the ship through unharmed. Naval officers view [Navigators](psychic-psyker-types.md) as little more than well-heeled civilians but they are accorded a level of respect and even deference aboard ship that can astound a planet-dweller.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 72`
