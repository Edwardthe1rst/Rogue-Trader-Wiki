Theatres  are  massive  military  conflicts  equivalent  to  Grand [Endeavours](economy-endeavours.md)  that  are  composed  of  numerous  campaigns  and brushfire wars. They can take upwards of a decade, contain 3-5 [Strategic Goals](mass-combat-strategic-goals.md), and can engulf numerous star systems within a given region of space. Extremely time and resource intensive, theatres cost 3-7 [Profit Factor](economy-wealth-and-acquisitions.md) and are a gamble for even the wealthiest Rogue Traders. Theatres require 2,400 Achievement Points to succeed, and can increase Profit Factor by up to 10.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 140`
