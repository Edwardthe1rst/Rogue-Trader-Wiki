When patrolling or intelligence shows a growing threat, Fleet Doctrine  is  to  aggressively  [Attack](combat-attack-rules.md)  it  with  whatever  ships  are available.  However, bringing an enemy to battle may not be viable until more powerful forces gather.  In that case, a series of raids are undertaken to keep the enemy off balance until the big guns arrive. In The Calixis Sector, raids are most likely to be undertaken at the request of the Holy Ordos. This might be to counter an identified xenos or heretic threat or to [Cover](combat-special-circumstances.md) the insertion and retrieval of Throne Agents into a hostile system.

Fast  ships  with  good  crews  are  needed  for  raiding. Each raid is different, a combination of speed, [Surprise](combat-surprise-rules.md) and daring intended to catch the enemy off-guard and seize the [Initiative](starship-combat-rules.md). Often raids target an enemy installation or patrol to provoke a reaction and set upon any relieving force as well. If  outnumbered, the [Raiders](ships-raiders-overview.md) use their superior speed to flee the area and draw headstrong pursuers into a waiting trap. Endless raid variants are planned and practised by aspiring commanders keen to show off their skills to their superiors.
