The kroothawk is a revered creature for the Kroot of Pech, and  its  image  is  often  worked  into  items  of  great  cultural or  spiritual  significance.  Many  Kroot  Shapers  carry  unique totems into battle, believing that they carry a special blessing that invokes the kroothawk's protection. A Shaper who carries a Kroothawk Totem gains the benefits of an additional Fate Point. However, this bonus Fate Point may only be spent, it may not be burnt for any reason.
