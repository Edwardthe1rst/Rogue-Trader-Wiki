Once the [Initiative](starship-combat-rules.md) order is determined and [Orders](combat-orders.md) are given, units take their Strategic [Turns](rules-combat-overview.md) in descending order, starting with the highest initiative. During a Strategic Turn, units may execute one Order. Any players embedded with a unit may also make extended actions to aid their unit.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 131`
