Warhead Swarm (Howler Cannon): When firing a Howler Cannon, each additional degree of success on the Ballistic Skill Test results in two additional hits instead of one, to a maximum of the weapon's Strength. Beam Cluster (Roarer Beam): When firing a [Roarer Beam](weapons-roarer-beam.md), every three additional  degrees  of  success  on  the  Ballistic  Skill  Test  results  in  two additional hits instead of one, to a maximum of the weapon's Strength. Spinal  Weapon  (Roarer  Beam): A  Roarer  Beam  may  only  be installed in a Prow Weapon Capacity Slot, and may only fire in the ship's forward arc.
