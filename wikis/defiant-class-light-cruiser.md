Crew: 61,500 crew, approx, 6,100 pilots and support [Staff](weapons-general.md), approx. Accel: 3.8 Gravities max sustainable acceleration

A rarer carrier variant of the Endeavour, the Defiant is rarely utilised on its own in The Imperial Navy, as its typical weapons fit leaves it vulnerable to direct [Attack](combat-attack-rules.md) and totally reliant upon its  relatively  small  number  of  Furies  and  Starhawks  to defend  it.  In  centuries  past,  these  ships  occasionally formed  the  flagships  of  long  range  light  recon [Patrols](patrols.md)  into  the  Koronus  Expanse,  prior  to  the establishment of the more permanent Passage Watch  27  Est.  Even  then,  some  Defiants still remain in service with 'Battlefleet Koronus.'

A few of these craft have made it into the hands of some richer Rogue Trader Dynasties, where they are often utilised as a versatile 'second ship,' a supplementary support vessel used to fill gaps in the capabilities of the Trader's primary flagship.
