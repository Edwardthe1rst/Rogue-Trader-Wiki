These vessels are rarely configured for [Combat](rules-combat-overview.md). Typically slow, [Unwieldy](weapons-general.md), and poorly armed, transports are not dangerous foes. It is a rare transport that carries enough firepower to significantly hinder an enemy vessel, and a rarer one still that can be anything but outmanoeuvred and outrun by true warships.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 117`
