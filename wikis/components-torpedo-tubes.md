## Gryphonne-pattern Torpedo Tubes

The most common template used for destroyers, [Raiders](ships-raiders-overview.md), and other smaller missile boats is the Gryphonne pattern. They are almost always mounted as a primary prow weapon. This Component is capable of firing four [Torpedoes](weapons-torpedoes.md) in each salvo. It can store 24 torpedoes, plus an additional four if the ship's [Captain](rank-captain.md) does not mind keeping four 'in the tubes.'

Volatile: If  this  Component is Damaged or Destroyed (but not Unpowered or Depressurised) while torpedoes are loaded in its tubes, it has a 10 percent chance of exploding. In this event, the Component is destroyed and the ship takes 2d5 [Hull](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) Integrity [Damage](character-injury.md).

## Voss-pattern Torpedo Tubes

The Voss [Forge World](chargen-stage2-origin-path.md) is known for the 'Voss Triumvirate'three  different  designs  of  light  [Cruisers](hulls-overview.md)  designed  as  heavy escorts for ships of the line and large convoys, as well as the smaller  Falchion-class  escort.  Most  Voss-pattern  ships  are armed with a pair of Torpedo Tubes to complement their other weaponry. These tubes are capable of firing two [Torpedoes](weapons-torpedoes.md) in each salvo. It can store 12 torpedoes, plus an additional two if the ship's [Captain](rank-captain.md) does not mind keeping two 'in the tubes.' Volatile: If this Component is Damaged or Destroyed (but not Unpowered or Depressurised) while torpedoes are loaded, it has a 10 percent chance of exploding. In this event, the Component is destroyed and the ship takes 2d5 [Hull](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) Integrity [Damage](character-injury.md).

## Fortis-pattern Torpedo Tubes

A modified variant of the Mars pattern, the Fortis pattern has comparable [Ammunition](economy-wealth-and-acquisitions.md) storage and rate of fire. Vessels with this Component are designed to fire their [Torpedoes](weapons-torpedoes.md) towards targets  at  an  accelerated  rate.  This  pattern  has  been  copied and is now used on refits at the Mars and Jovian shipyards. This  Component is capable of firing six torpedoes in each salvo. It can store 42 torpedoes, plus an additional six if the ship's [Captain](rank-captain.md) does not mind keeping six 'in the tubes.'

Enhanced Magnetic Coils: These torpedo tubes grant an additional +2VU speed to the torpedoes during the turn they are launched. Next turn, they return to their normal speed.

Volatile: If this Component is Damaged or Destroyed (but not Unpowered or Depressurised) while torpedoes are loaded, it has a 10 percent chance of exploding. In this event, the Component is destroyed and the ship takes 2d5 [Hull](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) Integrity [Damage](character-injury.md).
