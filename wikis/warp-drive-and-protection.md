[The Rak'gol](faction-rakgol-overview.md)'s [Warp Drives](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) are far more advanced than [The Xenos](faction-xenos-overview.md) races' other technology. Inspections of several hulked Rak'Gol vessels revealed [The Warp](warp-imperial-space-travel.md) [Drives](components-drives.md) seemed to have been added to the Rak'Gol ships independently of the starship's construction. This was further borne out by strange crystalline devices that make up part of the warp drives.

To  protect  against  the  predications  of  the  warp,  the Rak'Gol  seem  to  rely  exclusively  on  a  series  of  talismans, charms, and symbols built into the [Hulls](hulls-overview.md) of their vessels.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 98`
