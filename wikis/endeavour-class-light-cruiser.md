A design from the famous Voss [Forge World](chargen-stage2-origin-path.md), the Endeavourclass  light  [Cruiser](starship-anatomy-detailed.md)  has  a  markedly  different  mission  profile to the Dauntless. Given its relatively slow speed and heavier [Armour](armour.md), the Endeavour is typically used as a stopgap ship of the line, convoy flagship or battleship escort in gigantic set-piece naval battles. Endeavour [Captains](imperial-starship-types.md) must be careful not to bite off more than they can chew, for the ship lacks the firepower of a true cruiser, yet often finds itself in confrontations better suited  to  vessels  of  that  type.  Several  [Endeavours](economy-endeavours.md)  serve  in Battlefleet  Calixis,  as  there  is  proportionately  less  need  for true  ships  of  the  line;  many  routine  [Patrols](patrols.md)  in  the  Sector consist of an Endeavour accompanied by a pair of [Frigates](hulls-overview.md).
