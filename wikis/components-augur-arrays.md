While it would be impossible for [The Orks](faction-ork-overview.md) to voyage amongst the  stars  without  some  form  of  long-range  detection,  the nature of the technologies employed are unknown. As with much of Ork technology, a crude and simplistic appearance belies  impossible  sophistication.  Orks  simply  refer  to  their myriad  scanners  as  'searchy  grubbinz'  no  matter  whether they're powerful aetheric scanners or gigantic telescopes.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 77`
