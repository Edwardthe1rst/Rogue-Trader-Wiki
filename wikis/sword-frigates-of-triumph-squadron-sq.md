[Sword](weapons-general.md)-class frigates are similar to Lunar-class cruisers, in that both are the workhorses of countless battlefleets. While the Lunars bulk out the Battlefleet's ships of the line, the Swords undertake countless patrol and interdiction duties-hunting pirates, enforcing blockades, and protecting convoys. There are a dozen Swords in Triumph Squadron, and they are often spread across Battlefleet Koronus's areas of operations.

## Essential Components

## Supplemental Components

2 Dorsal Sunsear Mark 2 Laser Batteries: (Macrobattery, Strength 4,  [Damage](character-injury.md)  1d10+2,  Crit  4,  Range  6).  The  [Sword](weapons-general.md)  is  renowned  for the reliability of its laser batteries, and the Navy does not let civilians gain  access  to  them.  When  these  [Components](starship-anatomy-detailed.md)  become  damaged  or unpowered, roll 1d10. On a 4 or higher, the Component is unharmed. Extended  Supply  Vaults: Additional  supplies  allow  Swords  to undertake extended [Patrols](patrols.md).

Reinforced  Prow: Although  not  a  true  'armoured  prow,'  the Sword still possessed a reinforced adamantium beak.

## Special Rules and Modifier Summary

These modifiers apply to the Swords, taking [Components](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) into account:

- Ship may remain at void up to a year without suffering Crew Population or Morale loss.
- When performing [Extended Repairs](starship-travel-non-combat.md), repair 1 additional Hull Integrity .
- All  Command  Tests  and  Ballistic  Skill  Tests  made  to  fire  the ship's [Weapons](weapons-general.md) gain a +5 bonus.
