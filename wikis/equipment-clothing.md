## Blast Goggles

A  somewhat  unsuccessful  ammunitions  hive-factory  on  Sigurd  IV,  whose  owners  were  found  to  be  both  unwise  at business as well as in spiritual matters by the Inquisition. New ownership in the last century successfully converted the factories to producing rations and food packs. The existing machinery for refining and packing chemical propellants and explosives transitioned smoothly, and now the company imports vast quantities of agri-world products and turns them into processed meal kits. They are a staple of many survival packs and many portable habs even include them as part of the ready-assembly.
