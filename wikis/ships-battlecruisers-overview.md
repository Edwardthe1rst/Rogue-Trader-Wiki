Similar  to  [Cruisers](hulls-overview.md)  in  most  ways,  battlecruisers  are  typically more  heavily  armed,  mounting  additional  weaponry.  All  the advice  regarding  cruisers  applies  here,  though  their  heavier [Weapons](weapons-general.md)  load-out  makes  them  a  match  for  more  powerful enemies.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 117`
