Logistics objectives make up the bulk of any War Endeavour, and include all non-[Combat](rules-combat-overview.md) activities undertaken in support of  combat  operations  during  all  stages  of  the  Endeavour. Equivalent  to  a  standard  Endeavour's  [Objectives](economy-endeavours.md),  logistics objectives can include [Mustering Troops](mass-combat-mustering-troops.md), acquiring  war materiel, establishing trade routes and logistics chains, negotiating treaties, hiring mercenaries and the like.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 139`
