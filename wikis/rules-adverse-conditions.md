If the pilot wants to avoid incoming fire, he can jink, making his movements erratic and unpredictable to avoid incoming fire.  He  makes  a Challenging  (+0)  Piloting  Test .  On  a success, and for every addition degree of success, he imposes a -10 penalty on all shooting at and from his flyer until the beginning of his next turn.
