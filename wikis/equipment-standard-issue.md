For simplicity's sake, when outfitting troops, assume that the equipment purchased consists of a soldier's complete kit: his [Weapons](weapons-general.md), [Armour](armour.md), vehicles, and all other necessary [Gear](equipment-gear.md). When a soldier has Good quality gear, everything from his helmet to his boots, everything in his pockets, his weapon, and even his vehicle is of the same quality.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 128`
