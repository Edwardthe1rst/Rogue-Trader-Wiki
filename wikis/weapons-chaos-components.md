Unlike  the  earlier  entries  in  this  chapter,  the  forces  of Chaos  are-nominally  at  least-human,  and  their  ships are  constructed  in  the  same  manner  as  Imperial  vessels. Though some of their [Weapons](weapons-general.md) and [Components](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) may be ancient, even archeotech, they are not so different from their Imperial counterparts. Likewise, there are no overarching [Special Rules](mass-combat-special-rules.md) that pertain to the entire Chaos fleet.
