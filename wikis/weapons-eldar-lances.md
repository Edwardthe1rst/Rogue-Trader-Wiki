Similar in design to human [Lances](starship-supplemental-components.md), the Phantom Lance is used on smaller vessels as it requires less energy to power.

In contrast, the Pulsar Lance fires a burst of high-intensity laser bolts. The energy [Requirements](economy-endeavours.md) for this weapon are tremendous, but a single Pulsar Lance can strike multiple devastating blows.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 86`
