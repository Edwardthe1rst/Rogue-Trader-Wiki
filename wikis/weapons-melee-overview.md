This  alien  cutting-beam  weapon  is  thought  to  have  been discovered  in  the  Expanse  by  an  unknown  Rogue  Trader. 'Larn'  may  have  been  his  or  her  name,  or  a  ship,  or  the crewman  who  sold  them  first-no  one  knows  the  truth, though the Ordo Xenos is still investigating. It is thought that the Stryxis traders who sometimes deal in these devices know more  than  they  are  telling.  These  rifles  emit  an  ionisation beam which slices through most matter; despite their weight and recoil they are fearsome weapons for boarding parties to use against hulls or bulkheads.
