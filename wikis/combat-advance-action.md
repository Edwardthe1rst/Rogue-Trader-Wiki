An advancing unit moves their full  movement.  This  is  one  of the simplest [Orders](combat-orders.md), and is little more than moving a unit from one point to another. A unit that is advancing has packed up to move which reduces their [Combat](rules-combat-overview.md) effectiveness if they encounter enemy [Resistance](talents-descriptions.md) during their advance. See the '[On the Advance](combat-on-the-advance.md)' sidebar for information regarding moving and fighting.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 132`
