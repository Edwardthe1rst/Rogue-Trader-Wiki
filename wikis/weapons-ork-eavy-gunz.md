Alongside more conventional macrocannons, [The Orks](faction-ork-overview.md) commonly  employ  short-ranged  but  extremely  powerful guns.  Firing  dense  slugs  of  scrap  metal  massing  thousands of tonnes, as well as a jury-rigged [Plasma](weapons-general.md) bombs, these [Shells](weapons-ammunition.md) quickly  become  inaccurate  over  long  range.  Nevertheless, they cause terrifying [Damage](character-injury.md) at close range.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 77`
