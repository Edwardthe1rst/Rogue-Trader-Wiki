While the above [Orders](combat-orders.md) are designed for ground forces, many  are  for  use  by  air  forces  as  well.  The  Orders available to air forces are as follows: [Advance](combat-advance-action.md), [Cautious Advance](combat-cautious-advance.md),  [Disengage](starship-combat-rules.md)  (although  without  the  benefit  of digging-in  modifiers),  [Flank](combat-flank-action.md),  Forced  March  (Called Full  Throttle  when  attempted  by  air  forces),  [Strategic Withdrawal](mass-combat-strategic-withdrawal.md), [Attack](combat-attack-rules.md), [Charge](rules-combat-overview.md), [Feint](combat-feint-action.md), and [Push Through](combat-push-through-action.md). Certain Flash-points may involve Air Forces as well.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 135`
