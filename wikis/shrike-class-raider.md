The Meritech clans were an infamous rabble of voidborn stargypsies,  wanderers  and  scavengers,  who  came  to  reside  in  the border zone worlds of the Merates cluster during the final years of  the  40th  millennia.  Initially  content  to  forage  for  parts  for their ramshackle fleets from ruined [Space Hulks](warp-space-hulks.md) drifting into their territory , within a century they had embarked upon a lifestyle of piracy, and routinely attacked vessels passing between the Calixis and Ixaniad Sectors. In 211.M41 mere banditry gave way to allout  war  when  Meritech  [Raiders](ships-raiders-overview.md)  ambushed  a  trio  of  Battlefleet Calixis [Sword](weapons-general.md)-class [Frigates](hulls-overview.md). Only one Sword escaped.

This plunged the entire region into a fifteen-year war which only concluded when Battlefleet Calixis crushed the Meritech Clan  strongholds. Subsequent  Inquisitorial investigations established, it is said, sinister tech-heretics behind the events.

Many of the deadly raider vessels utilised by the clanfleet survived,  sold  to  pirates  and  other  rogues  to  ensure  safe passage  for  the  upper  echelons  of  the  [Renegade](chargen-stage2-origin-path.md)  clanlords. The vessel designated a Shrike class is typical of the Meritech ships  of  the  period,  and  indeed  formed  the  main  strength of  its  bandit  [Squadrons](squadrons-overview.md).  A  lithe,  incredibly  advanced  vessel that utilised heretical technologies unknown to The Adeptus Mechanicus, few Rogue Traders are able to maintain these ships at their original performance levels, given their bizarrely innovative heretech, but they remain dangerous fast raiders.
