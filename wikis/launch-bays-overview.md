As with other species' fleets, only larger vessels normally have the space and resources necessary to support [Attack Craft](attack-craft-rules.md). Carved from the flanks of a [Kroozer](kroozer.md), launch tubes and landing decks sit open to the void but for the crackling force fields that hold the ship's atmosphere in. They are almost always crammed full of Fighta-bommas and Assault Boats. The remaining nooks are claimed by the swaggering and insane Speed Freaks who pilot the  [Attack](combat-attack-rules.md)  craft,  their  hordes  of  Grots,  and  Meks  constantly building, rebuilding, and 'improving' the fighters.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 78`
