A unit that is moving from one place to another can find their orderly [Advance](combat-advance-action.md) interrupted by a [Surprise](combat-surprise-rules.md) encounter with an enemy unit. A unit under the Advance Order is considered to be packed for moving and not operating at full readiness. Any unit that encounters an enemy unit during an Advance may break off their advance to fight, but  are  caught  off-guard,  and  roll  1  less  [Damage](character-injury.md)  dice during the remainder of their Strategic Turn.

unit is treated as Surprised for the remainder of the Strategic Turn as they expected the enemy to be somewhere else entirely.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 133`
