Also  sometimes  called  a  group  [Commander](rank-commander.md),  the  rank  of commodore was originally  only  a  temporary  one  given  to a  senior  [Captain](rank-captain.md)  placed  in  [Charge](rules-combat-overview.md)  of  a  squadron  of  ships. Over time the rank of commodore has found its way into permanent  usage  as  what  were  once  temporary  [Squadrons](squadrons-overview.md) stabilised into regular patrol routes and areas of responsibility. On the rare  and  terrible  occasions  when  capital  ships  join together in squadrons the senior captain is still promoted to commodore for the duration of the engagement.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 69`
