When a psyker reaches deeper into [The Warp](warp-imperial-space-travel.md) to power his abilities, there is always the chance of the empyrean bleeding in  to  our  reality,  a  factor  that  is  invariably  destructive  and damaging. Sometimes this expression of [The Warp](warp-imperial-space-travel.md) is unique to  the  psyker,  but  in  general,  it  is  as  unpredictable  as  the warp itself. Many possible effects have been noted including dramatic temperature drops, ghostly voices, feelings of unease, or the local vegetation dying all at once. In very rare instances, full scale warp breaches with the direst of consequences can occur. When a psyker uses his power at the Unfettered level or greater, he risks generating psychic phenomena. See [Focus Power](psychic-techniques-list.md) and Table 6-3: Psychic Strength for details. Power and Table 6-3: Psychic Strength for details.

[Astropaths](psychic-psyker-types.md) are an invaluable addition to any ship that traverses the voids, far from inhabited planets or space stations or any other relay for mundane communications. The Astropath's ability to send messages over interstellar distances  makes  his  presence  aboard  a  Rogue  Trader's  ship  almost  mandatory.  However,  Rogue  Traders  that frequently delve into uncharted regions such as the Koronus Expanse often carry more than one [Soul-bound](character-traits.md) Psyker on board, as having an Astropath at one's side can be very profitable when dealing with inscrutable xenos or [Primitive](weapons-general.md) human tribes.
