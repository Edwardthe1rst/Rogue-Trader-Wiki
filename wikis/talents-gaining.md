'Mediocrity nests among the countless masses, but true talent rests in the genius of the select few.'

-Administratum Magister Ylva Geraint P layers receive or choose between a number of Talents during the creation of their characters in Chapter I: Character  Creation ,  but  will  gain  many  more  by spending  Experience  Points  as  they  [Advance](combat-advance-action.md)  through  their [Career](chargen-stage2-origin-path.md) Path, as outlined in Chapter Ii: Career Paths . To add fun and challenge to the process, the [Acquisition](economy-acquisition-rules.md) of Talents both during creation and over time can match the player's character concept and his in-game experience throughout the [Campaign](rules-campaign.md) in addition to simply following his [Career](chargen-stage2-origin-path.md) Path.



*Source:* `Roguetrader Corerulebook, page 91`
