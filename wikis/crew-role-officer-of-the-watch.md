The  officer  of  the  watch  draws  up  duty  rosters,  assigns watches and attends the ship's chronometers. On most ships the title of officer of the watch is rotated through the ranking [Commissioned Officers](ranks-commissioned-officers.md). On older vessels it tends to become a permanent position assigned to the officer that has served the longest time aboard the ship.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 72`
