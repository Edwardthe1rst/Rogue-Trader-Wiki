## Weapons

In the Adeptus Astartes, Space Marine Scouts use this lightly armed and armoured vehicle to aid in long range reconnaissance and hitand-run raids. The Scout Bike is a tough and robust machine, able to traverse almost any terrain and easy to repair and maintain. It is also armed with twin-linked bolters or a grenade launcher and targeter arrays allowing the weapons to be fired on the move-granting the rider impressive firepower. These traits make them popular amongst Explorers with enough influence to acquire them.
