These [Weapons](weapons-general.md) are even more feared by starship [Captains](imperial-starship-types.md) than standard  [Torpedoes](weapons-torpedoes.md).  Mercifully  rare  and  extremely  brutal, melta warheads detonate a precisely-organised series of melta charges  upon  impact  with  a  target,  burning  through  [Hull](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) plating and consuming sections of the vessel's interior in a roiling conflagration.

A hit from a Melta Torpedo that deals [Damage](character-injury.md) automatically causes a Fire! Critical Result, in addition to any other Crits is  may  cause.  Carrying  these  extremely  volatile  weapons is  nearly  as  dangerous for a vessel as it is for its targets. If [Torpedo Tubes](components-torpedo-tubes.md) armed with these [Warheads](weapons-warheads.md) are destroyed, one [Additional Components](starship-additional-components.md) of the GM's choice is set on fire.

Terminal  Penetration  (4): When  rolling  damage  for  this weapon, re-roll any dice results of 4 or lower. The results of the second roll stand.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 9`
