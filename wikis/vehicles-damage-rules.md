In some  cases, flyers  may  be operating in adverse circumstances, such as stormy weather. In this case, the GM should increase the difficulty of all Piloting Tests by one step (or force pilots to make a Challenging (+0) Piloting Test if they would not normally have to test), and impose a -10 to  all  shooting.  These  penalties  should  be  increased  if  the conditions are especially bad.



*Source:* `Into the Storm, page 177`
