When an [Armour](armour.md) unit digs in (see the [Orders](combat-orders.md) section on page 132 for details on digging in) they are said to be '[Hull](starship-anatomy-detailed.md) down.' What this typically means is that the vehicle is hidden somewhere where only the barrel of its main cannon is exposed, and the majority of the hull and turret  are  in  [Cover](combat-special-circumstances.md).  When  an  armoured  unit  goes hull down, they gain an additional 1d10 points of cover.

*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 123`
