Space [Combat](rules-combat-overview.md) contains factors beyond the [Hulls](hulls-overview.md) and [Weapons](weapons-general.md) of the combatants. Crew skill can often mean the difference  between  victory  and  defeat,  granting  additional reliable actions and greater chances of success. This is a valid consideration for the players, as a skilled crew can supplement the  Explorers'  abilities  and  make  them  more  effective  in general, but for [Npc Vessels](ships-npc-vessels.md), this is a crucial consideration, as  Crew  Quality  is  the  main  factor  that  influences  how effectively  a  starship  can  be  used.  Skilled  NPC  crews  gain more from [Extended Actions](starship-combat-rules.md) or [Orders](combat-orders.md), hit more frequently, board more effectively, and manoeuvre more deftly. A small number  of  [Raiders](ships-raiders-overview.md)  with  Veteran  (50)  Crews  will  be  more effective at attacking and eluding their enemies than a larger number of raiders with Competent (30) Crew.

[Additional Components](starship-additional-components.md), such as the [Munitorium](starship-supplemental-components.md) or Teleportarium  either  improve  the  effectiveness of  standard methods, or allow for new  tactics, while unconventional strategies  such  as  boarding  and  ramming  grant  additional methods of harming foes at extreme close range. Particularly in the case of enemies like [The Orks](faction-ork-overview.md), boarding actions and ramming are effective tactics which are difficult to defend against.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 119`
