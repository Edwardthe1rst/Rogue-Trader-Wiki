The master of arms is charged with ensuring that all weaponry onboard is serviceable and [Ready](rules-combat-overview.md) for action, from the greatest macrocannon to the meanest stub pistol. The sergeants-at-arms report directly to him, and by extension all armsmen are under his control, and so he also performs a role akin to chief of police aboard ship. The master of arms is normally appointed from among the [Commissioned Officers](ranks-commissioned-officers.md) on a ship but more rarely a senior warrant officer may hold this position. Experience with The Imperial Guard or Adeptus Arbites is thought to be a great benefit for officers performing this role, and a master of arms will  often  seize  any  opportunities  they  can  to  participate  in landing parties and other joint operations.



*Source:* `Battle Fleet of the Koronus, page 72`
